Why is the Notified Body shortage a problem?

Only 50-60 qualified ISO 42001 consultants globally, against tens of thousands of enterprises needing certification before Q2-Q3 2026. Companies that wait will face 6-9 month queues. Council of AI's pre-certification gap analysis dramatically shortens the actual Notified Body engagement — we do the prep work in 48 hours, you arrive at the NB with a clean roadmap.

Is this a substitute for an actual Notified Body audit?

No. Council of AI produces pre-certification evidence + remediation roadmaps that the Notified Body accepts as preparation. You still need the formal certification audit from an accredited body. Think of us as the Big 4 "readiness service" — but automated, signed, and 100× cheaper.

What's a "cryptographically signed attestation"?

Every audit we produce is signed with HMAC-SHA256 and carries a public verify URL. Your auditor, board, or procurement team can validate it at meok-attestation-api.vercel.app/verify/<cert_id> without contacting us. Tamper-evident by construction. 365-day validity.

Can Pro subscribers verify certs offline?

Yes. pip install meok-attestation-verify — zero-dependency Python tool, pipe the cert JSON on stdin, get VALID or INVALID back.
