# Competitive Intelligence: RegGeoInt Market Landscape

## Executive Summary

The AI governance market is fragmenting into three distinct battlegrounds:
1. **Enterprise Governance SaaS** ($890M→$5.8B by 2029, 45% CAGR)
2. **AI Security & Compliance** ($8.5B→$32B by 2030, 35% CAGR)
3. **AI Companions / Characters** ($36.8B→$318B by 2033, 31% CAGR)

**No competitor operates across all three.** MEOK's RegGeoInt + Character Infrastructure + Trust Layer is the only platform spanning governance, geospatial compliance, and character marketplace.

## Tier 1 Competitors: Direct AI Governance

### Credo AI
- **Founded:** 2020, Palo Alto
- **Funding:** $39.3M (Series A1: $21M July 2024)
- **Employees:** 66
- **Revenue:** $100K-$5M (est.)
- **Investors:** Booz Allen Ventures, Mozilla Ventures, Sands Capital, FPV Ventures
- **Weakness:** No geospatial mapping. Policy packs only. No character infrastructure.
- **Threat Level:** 🟡 Medium — well-funded but narrow scope

### Norm AI
- **Founded:** 2023, New York
- **Funding:** $136.1M (largest in category)
- **Employees:** 100-250
- **Revenue:** $25M-$100M (est.)
- **Weakness:** Regulatory interpretation only. No deployment tooling. No map.
- **Threat Level:** 🟡 Medium — well-funded but legal-tech focused

### Holistic AI
- **Founded:** 2022, Palo Alto (DeepMind alumni)
- **Funding:** $200M Series A (May 2024, $370M valuation)
- **Investors:** Accel, UiPath, Schmidt Family, Mozilla Ventures
- **Focus:** Multi-agent AI governance platform
- **Weakness:** Enterprise-only. No geospatial. No government/defense vertical.
- **Threat Level:** 🟡 Medium — DeepMind pedigree but narrow enterprise focus

### Arthur AI
- **Founded:** 2019, New York
- **Funding:** $60M+ (Series B: $42M Sep 2022)
- **Focus:** ML observability, bias detection, performance monitoring
- **Weakness:** Model-centric only. No regulatory compliance. No deployment governance.
- **Threat Level:** 🟢 Low — adjacent category (MLOps, not governance)

### 2021.AI / Saidot / Anch.ai / Sprinto
- **Combined funding:** ~$50M
- **Focus:** SME compliance checklists, EU AI Act templates
- **Weakness:** No API-first architecture. No real-time mapping. Manual workflows.
- **Threat Level:** 🟢 Low — template vendors, not platforms

- **Weakness:** No compliance infrastructure. No B2B. No API for developers.

- **Revenue:** $48M ARR (Oct 2025), up from $30M (Apr 2025)
- **Employees:** 12 ($2.5M rev/employee — most efficient)
- **Weakness:** No compliance. No B2B marketplace. Consumer chat only.
