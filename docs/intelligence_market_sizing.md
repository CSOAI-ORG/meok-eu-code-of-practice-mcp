# Market Sizing: RegGeoInt Revenue Architecture

## Bottom-Up Market Analysis

### Methodology
Instead of accepting analyst TAM figures at face value, we built a bottom-up model based on:
1. Addressable entities (governments, enterprises, AI systems)
2. Willingness to pay per entity
3. MEOK's technical ability to serve

## Key Assumptions & Risks

### Assumptions
1. EU AI Act enforcement drives demand (Aug 2026 deadline)
2. US state patchwork continues (no federal preemption)
3. AI companion market grows 30%+ CAGR
4. MEOK achieves product-market fit by Q2 2026
5. Network effects from marketplace drive vertical adoption

### Risks
| Risk | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|------------|------------|

| AI companion market crashes | 20% | Pivot to enterprise-only |
| Regulatory harmonization | 15% | Pivot to audit/certification |
| Palantir builds mapping | 25% | Partner, don't compete directly |

## The $1B+ Path

Base scenario reaches $663M by 2030. To cross $1B:
1. **Defense contracts:** Single NATO theater contract = $5-10M/year
2. **Insurance integration:** D&O carriers require compliance data = $50M/year
3. **Platform revenue share:** 15% of character revenue across ecosystems
4. **Data licensing:** Real-time regulatory feeds to Bloomberg, Reuters = $30M/year

**The path to $1B is credible if execution matches infrastructure quality.**
