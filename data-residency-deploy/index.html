The headline

MEOK stores all customer data, audit data, signing keys, and attestation payloads on UK infrastructure. The keystone is hosted on Vercel (sovereign-UK region, no data leaves the UK). The keystone signing keys are stored in Vercel environment variables, encrypted at rest. MEOK does not transfer customer data outside the UK without explicit written consent.

EU AI Act Article 10 compliance

EU AI Act Article 10 requires:

  • Training data quality + relevance + representativeness
  • Data governance + data preparation
  • Bias detection + mitigation
  • Data provenance documentation

MEOK's posture:

  • No PII stored on the keystone — the keystone signs attestations about your AI systems; it doesn't store the data those systems process.
  • No model weights stored on the keystone — the keystone doesn't train or fine-tune models.
  • Audit trail is HMAC-chained — every sign/verify event is hash-linked, immutable, queryable for 7 years.
  • Transparency log — every signed attestation is anchored to a public log, so customers can prove their attestation existed as of a specific date.

What we do for Enterprise customers who need multi-region

  • EU mirror: Replicate the keystone signing key to an EU region (Frankfurt, Dublin) for EU customers. ~2 week deployment. £1,499/mo Enterprise tier.
  • US mirror: Same, US region (us-east-1, us-west-2). For US enterprise customers. ~2 week deployment. £1,499/mo Enterprise tier.
  • On-prem: Full on-prem deployment on the customer's infrastructure. ~4 week deployment. £1,499/mo Enterprise tier + £20K setup fee.

Where we don't compromise

  • We don't store customer data on US infrastructure by default (EU AI Act Article 10 friendly).
  • We don't train on customer data (OpenAI, Anthropic, Mistral all confirm zero-retention in their TOS).
  • We don't log LLM requests to persistent storage (logged in-memory only, never written to disk).
  • We don't share signing keys with third parties.
