### The Five Tiers

**🜁 TIER 1 — IDEA ($0.07 / notarization)**
Single-claim, single-jurisdiction, hash-only stamp. The whisper. The minimum covenant. Built for the garage inventor who needs to know *the chain heard them*.

**🜂 TIER 2 — CLAIM ($0.35 / notarization)**
Up to 10 claims, prior-art silent search, 12-month ledger retention. The workshop. Built for the indie who is iterating fast and needs claim-by-claim witness.

**🜃 TIER 3 — FILE ($4.50 / notarization)**
Provisional specification draft, PCT routing preparation, 7-year retention, jurisdiction election. The forge. Built for the SMB R&D team ready to file in 14 minutes.

**🜄 TIER 4 — DEFEND ($24 / notarization)**
Full Hivelock proof, sovereign escrow, USPTO/EPO/JPO/CNIPA/IPOS routing, prior-art defense package, 99-year retention. The shield. Built for the solo patent attorney defending a client's priority.

**🜅 TIER 5 — SOVEREIGN ($240 / notarization)**
Includes everything in Tier 4 plus: 33-agent BFT redundant notarization, treaty-route optimization, opposition defense kit, perpetual sovereign escrow, named human notary. The crown. Built for the university TTO, the corporate IP department, and the legendary solo inventor.
