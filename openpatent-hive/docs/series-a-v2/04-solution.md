### Architecture of Memory

OpenPatent.ai replaces silence with notarized presence. A whispered idea enters the hive and exits as a **chain-verified, time-stamped, jurisdictionally portable disclosure asset** — in under 14 minutes, in 9 languages, on a 33-agent BFT consensus backbone.

The spine is six layers. Each layer is a covenant. Each covenant is a lock.

---

### The Six Layers

**L1 — THE VAULT (Sealed Origin)**
End-to-end encrypted origin chamber. The idea is hashed, sealed, and never revealed in plaintext to any party below the disclosure threshold chosen by the inventor. Default: silent until the inventor unseals.

**L2 — THE FIVE `.ai` DOMAIN GATEWAYS (Witnessed Surfaces)**
`idea.ai · claim.ai · prior.ai · file.ai · defend.ai` — five sovereign entry points, each specialized for a stage of the invention lifecycle. Each gateway is itself a notarization witness.

**L3 — THE PATENTMCP CHAIN (Notarized Custody)**
The 23-tool Model Context Protocol surface writes the disclosure to a notarized chain. 146 entries live today. Each entry is a covenant-stamped event the world can verify without seeing the idea.

**L4 — THE HIVELOCK PROOF (Cryptographic Sovereignty)**
A BFT-validated, threshold-signed proof of existence, authorship, and timestamp. Survives forks. Survives corporate dissolution. Survives the death of the inventor.

**L5 — THE SOVEREIGN ESCROW (Jurisdictional Bridge)**
The disclosure is mirrored to jurisdiction-specific notaries (USPTO, EPO, JPO, CNIPA, IPOS) per the inventor's election, with automatic treaty-route mapping (PCT, Paris Convention).

**L6 — THE NOTARY STAMP (Public Presence)**
Optional public disclosure with redaction controls — the invention is provably yours, provably prior, and provably yours to license, sell, or defend. The chain has spoken.
