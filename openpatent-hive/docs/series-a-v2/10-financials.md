### Assumptions

- **PAYG notarization** is the sole revenue primitive
- **Blended ASP** rises from $1.40 (Y1) to $2.10 (Y5) via tier mix shift
- **Gross margin** steady at 91% (chain gas + MCP compute is the only COGS)
- **R&D** held flat at 28% of revenue (decreases as % of revenue but absolute $ grows)
- **S&M** front-loaded Y1–Y2 (38%→24%) to acquire the 5 `.ai` domain funnels
- **No equity raises** beyond Series A in the modeled horizon
