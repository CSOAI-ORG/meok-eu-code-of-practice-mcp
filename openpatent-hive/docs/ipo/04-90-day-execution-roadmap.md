## The Critical Path

The above schedule assumes you have:
- `gh auth login` working (5 minutes to enable)
- Namecheap API key in env (5 minutes to set)
- 1 GPU sovereign cluster (existing)
- 1 enterprise pilot willing to be a design partner (3-7 days of outreach)

If you only have **some** of these by Day 1:
- No GitHub → defer publishing to Week 2 (lose 7 days of organic discovery)
- No Namecheap → can't get HTTPS → lose enterprise pilots (no credibility)
- No enterprise pilot → defer BFT council demo → Phase 2 slips

The critical path is: **GitHub publish → HTTPS → first paying user → first enterprise pilot → Series A**.

If you hit the critical path, you hit the hockey stick.

## What I would NOT do in the first 90 days

- ❌ Tokenize the domain portfolio. **Defer to Month 6.** Legal costs (£50K+), tax complexity, regulatory overhead. Wait until you have 5+ developed domains.
- ❌ Build a sales team. **Defer to Month 6.** Outsource to 3 fractional sales consultants on success fee only.
- ❌ File a patent on PatentMCP. **Defer to Month 9.** Defensive publication is sufficient. Patent costs £15K+ and 2-3 years.
- ❌ Hire a CTO. **Defer to Month 6-9.** You are the CTO. The first engineering hire is a senior infra engineer who complements you, not a CTO who replaces you.
- ❌ Build humanoid-robot integrations. **Defer to Month 12+.** Market is nascent. Build the rest of the stack first.
