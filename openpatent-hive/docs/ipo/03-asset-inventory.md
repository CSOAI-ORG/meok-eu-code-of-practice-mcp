## The Top 5 (Develop in Next 90 Days)

These are the domains where investment × return is highest:

### #1: grabhire.ai — Crane/permit hire for AI agents in construction

- **Why:** Construction is the largest physical-economy vertical (£1.7T/year in UK alone). AI agents will be hiring cranes before they're hiring accountants. The domain is category-defining.
- **MCP capabilities:** Permit check, operator vetting, crane-type matching, location-aware availability, price negotiation
- **Kill shot:** One construction firm using the MCP to book a crane hire end-to-end. £1K MRR.
- **Valuation with live MCP + 100+ transactions/month:** £200K-£500K (10-100x registration)

### #2: muckaway.ai — Waste logistics for construction sites

- **Why:** Every construction site generates waste. The current booking process is fax/phone/email. AI agents will automate this first.
- **MCP capabilities:** Skip-hire scheduling, waste-type classification, permit-check, route optimization, payment
- **Kill shot:** Cross-sell from grabhire.ai (every grabhire job generates a muckaway job). £5K MRR.
- **Valuation with live MCP + 100+ transactions/month:** £200K-£500K

### #3: openpatent.ai (already live) — IP protection for AI-assisted inventions

- **Why:** This is the moat for all other domains. Every invention, dataset, and process on every other domain is protected through openpatent.ai.
- **Status:** ✅ LIVE. 9/10 services healthy on sovereign VM.
- **Kill shot:** First 5 paying customers, £1K MRR, 90 days.
- **Valuation:** £10M-£20M Phase 1, £100M+ Phase 2 (see IPO narrative)

### #4: microgreens.ai — Microgreens operation AI (own dataset from farm)

- **Why:** You have the only commercial microgreens dataset in the world (from iokfarm.co.uk). This is **unfair** data advantage.
- **MCP capabilities:** Yield prediction, harvest scheduling, climate control, packaging, distribution
- **Kill shot:** 5 commercial microgreens operations using the MCP. £1K MRR.
- **Valuation:** £50K-£200K (data + working MCP)

### #5: councilof.ai — AI governance council-as-a-service

- **Why:** Every regulated industry needs a 33-agent-style BFT council to make compliance decisions. The 33-agent BFT council we built for openpatent.ai becomes the prototype.
- **MCP capabilities:** Decision voting, evidence aggregation, audit trail, regulatory citation
- **Kill shot:** White-label the BFT council for 3 enterprises. £5K-£10K MRR.
- **Valuation:** £200K-£1M (governance is sticky, high-margin)

## Why this asset inventory is unique

Most domain investors hold names as **passive appreciating assets**. The carry is a sunk cost, and they hope for an aftermarket sale. Domain aftermarket sales (DNJournal): 1-2% of held domains per year, average sale price £2K-£50K.

We are different because:
1. **27 .ai domains are namespace + product**, not just names. Each has a specific MCP server planned.
2. **We have the physical base** (iokfarm.co.uk farm) that provides real data for the agricultural/aquaculture domains — a moat no purely-digital competitor has.
3. **We have the governance layer** (CSOAI) that gives every developed domain a compliance wrapper. Competitors would need to build this from scratch.
4. **We have the sovereign infrastructure** (35.242.143.249 VM) that runs every developed domain. We are not paying AWS margins.
5. **We have 313+ additional MCPs** in the SOV3 bridge that interlock with the 27 .ai domains.

This is not a domain portfolio. It is a **sovereign AI infrastructure company that happens to own the namespace**.
