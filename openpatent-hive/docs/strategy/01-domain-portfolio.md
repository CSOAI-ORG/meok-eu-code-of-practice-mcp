A domain bought today at £2,400 is worth £6,400 in Y1, £17,000 in Y2, **£45,500 in Y3** at pure TLD beta alone — before any power-pack premium.

2. **Power-pack premium.** Each domain embedded in a power pack (4 of them, see `02-industry-power-packs.md`) earns a **vertical dominance premium** of 3–8× over TLD beta alone.
