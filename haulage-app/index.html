Why one platform?

UK trade businesses don't operate in silos. A waste-management firm runs muckaway lorries (need O-Licence), tips muck at a recycling site (need EA waste carrier reg), drops a grab on a building site (need NRSWA accreditation), and dispatches a skip the same week (need a Section 34 transfer note). One operator. Four regulators. Today they juggle four spreadsheets. Haulage.app gives them one toolbox.
