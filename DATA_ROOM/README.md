## The principle of this data room: proof over claims

This raise is built to be the **opposite of the startups that raised on lies**. MEOK's own product is cryptographic proof-of-compliance. We raise the same way: **every line in here is independently verifiable by a stranger who does not trust us.** Where the proof does not yet exist, the README says `NEED` — out loud. We do not dress aspiration as fact. An empty folder is honest; an inflated number is not.

Three numbers a skeptic will check in an afternoon, stated before they find them:
- **MRR is £0.** The products are live and can take cards; they have not yet sold. This is our single biggest gap and we lead with it.
- **422 GitHub repos, max 2 stars on any one.** Volume is not traction. The repos are IP depth, not adoption — we never pitch them as adoption.
- **271 MCPs published on PyPI / 316 built.** Not "410+", not "294". The real number, verifiable with one script.

## Status legend (used in every folder README)

- **HAVE** — the document/evidence exists and is verifiable today. A link, a file, or a public record.
- **IN-PROGRESS** — partially exists or is being assembled; what's missing is named.
- **NEED** — does not exist yet. This is a to-do, stated honestly. (At pre-seed, several `NEED`s are normal and expected.)
