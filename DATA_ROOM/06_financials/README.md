# 06 — Financials

The "what are the actual numbers?" folder. At pre-seed with £0 MRR, honesty here means: thin, clean, and clear about what's company vs. founder.

| # | Document | Why an investor needs it | Status | Source / how to verify |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 06.1 | Statutory accounts (CSOAI LTD) | Even a first/dormant set shows the company is being run properly. | **IN-PROGRESS** | First accounts due per the incorporation date (check Companies House #16939677 for the due date). File on time; place the filed PDF here. |
| 06.2 | Management P&L (current) | Shows the burn and that there is essentially no revenue yet — stated, not hidden. | **NEED** | Build a simple monthly P&L (Xero/QuickBooks). Expect it to show ~£0 revenue and modest costs. That's fine and honest at pre-seed. |
| 06.3 | Xero / accounting-system setup | Demonstrates audit-readiness from day one (needed for any later AIM path anyway). | **NEED** | Set up Xero or QuickBooks; connect the business bank account (see `01_corporate/`). |
| 06.4 | Business bank statement | Proves no personal/business commingling — a valuation-killer if absent. | **NEED** | Open Mettle/Tide/Starling, route all flows through it, place a redacted statement header here. |
| 06.5 | Founder-business revenue evidence (context) | Templeman Opticians is real trading cashflow — it anchors founder credibility and de-risks "can this person run a business". | **HAVE (separate entity)** | ~£2.5–5K/mo (per memory). Verify via Companies House + bank statements for the optical business. **Clearly labelled as a separate company, not CSOAI revenue** — never blended into MEOK's numbers. |
| 06.6 | Financial model / projections | Shows the path from £0 → first MRR → seed-stage trigger; assumptions visible and conservative. | **NEED** | Build a simple 18–24mo model anchored on the £29–£999 ladder. Mark every assumption as an assumption. Tie to deck slide 12. |
| 06.7 | SEIS/EIS eligibility note | Confirms the company qualifies and what relief the angel gets — often the deal-decider for UK angels. | **NEED** | Document SEIS (if within early-stage limits — typically <2yrs trading, <£200K gross assets, <25 employees) then EIS. Pair with HMRC Advance Assurance in `01_corporate/01.8`. State limits as the rules, not promises; the investor's own adviser confirms relief. |

**Honesty flags:**
- **Do not blend Templeman Opticians revenue into CSOAI's numbers.** It is a separate company. It is context/credibility (06.5), not MEOK traction. Blending them would be misleading.
- The P&L will show ~£0 revenue. Present it as-is. The whole credibility of this raise is that the numbers are real, including the uncomfortable ones.
- EIS/SEIS relief is **subject to HMRC and the investor's own circumstances** — present the rules, never guarantee the relief.
