Case Study: UK Domiciliary Care

A 3-day audit, a signed CQC-ready attestation, and a network effect that unlocked a competitor sale.

📍 UK · Care Homes 🏥 ~2,800 care visits/day · 14 LAs ⏱ 3 days 💡 Network effect unlocked

The buyer

A UK-based domiciliary care operator, ~2,800 care visits/day across 14 local authorities. CQC-registered. Operates an AI-assisted falls-risk prediction system for elderly clients.

The risk they faced

The signed attestation became a sales tool, not just a compliance document.
