Who we are

What we do

We deliver 4-day signed Ed25519 attestations for the EU AI Act (Article 50 + Article 6 + Article 9 + Annex III + Annex IV), the UK AI Bill, the Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA), the Network and Information Security Directive 2 (NIS2), ISO 42001, the GDPR Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA), HIPAA, the SOC 2 Trust Service Criteria, the NIST AI Risk Management Framework, the AISI voluntary commitments, the Montreal Declaration for Responsible AI, and the Toronto Declaration.

What we do not do

  • We are not a law firm. We do not provide legal advice. Our 30-min legal review is delivered by a separate UK-qualified AI policy partner.
  • We are not a certification body. We do not issue ISO 42001 certs (those come from CB-registered firms). We issue signed Ed25519 attestations.
  • We are not a regulator. We do not enforce the EU AI Act or the UK AI Bill. We sign the cert; the regulator enforces.
