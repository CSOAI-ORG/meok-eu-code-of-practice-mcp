**YORKSHIRE UK, 14 June 2026** — MEOK AI Labs (CSOAI Ltd UK 16939677) today launched the MEOK Gaming hive, the first COAI (Certified Operational AI)-certified gaming AI infrastructure. The hive comprises three standalone MCP (Model Context Protocol) servers:

- **`@csoai-org/blizzard-wow-mcp`** — 10 tools for World of Warcraft auction house intelligence, market data, cross-realm arbitrage, crafting profit, guild analytics, farming route optimizer
- **`@csoai-org/mmoagent-mcp`** — 6 cross-game intelligence tools covering 6 MMOs (WoW, FFXIV, EVE, OSRS, PoE, Diablo IV)
- **`@csoai-org/evergame-hive-mcp`** — 4 data-moat tools with SOV3-enabled sovereign data export (your data, your control, JSON/CSV/Parquet on demand)

All 20 tools ship with the **COAI/INTELLIGENCE_ONLY** compliance gate — no automation, no botting, no client interaction. Every query leaves a hash-chained HMAC-signed audit trail anchored to the MEOK Attestation API (meok-attestation-api.vercel.app, v1.2.0).

**"We provide intelligence. You provide the clicks."** — the MEOK Gaming Ethical AI Manifesto

**Why now**: the EU AI Act Article 50 cliff (2 August 2026) requires AI-generated content to carry provenance signals and watermarks. Gaming AI vendors without an audit trail will be shut out. MEOK Gaming ships with that audit trail from day one.

**Governance**: the MEOK Gaming BFT (Byzantine Fault Tolerant) Council — 5 community voters + 1 chair — votes on every new tool proposal. The voting question: *"Does this feature violate any game's Terms of Service?"* `wow_auto_posting` is the canonical REJECTED example.
