## Description
MEOK is a fleet of 340+ standalone MCP (Model Context Protocol) servers implementing privacy and AI governance tooling.

**Direct relevance to IAPP**:
- `gdpr-compliance-ai-mcp` — Article 30 records, Article 22 automated decision-making audit, Article 33 breach notification, DPIA automation, data subject request workflow
- `eu-ai-act-compliance-mcp` — 410 articles including data-protection-by-design (Art 25), fundamental rights impact assessment (Art 27)
- `ccpa-ai-mcp` — California Consumer Privacy Act automation
- `pipeda-ai-mcp` — Canadian Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act
- `lgpd-ai-mcp` — Brazil Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados
- `dpdpa-ai-mcp` — India Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023

**Cross-border data transfer**:
- `scc-2021-mcp` — Standard Contractual Clauses automation
- `tia-mcp` — Transfer Impact Assessment tooling

**Why it matters for privacy professionals**:
- The fleet converts regulatory text into runnable, deterministic tools
- Every assessment leaves a hash-chained HMAC-signed audit trail
- The `/verify` meter at proofof.ai/verify is public, unauthenticated, and auditor-friendly
