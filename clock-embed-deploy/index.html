The 1-line embed

Add this anywhere in your HTML:

And place this <div id="meok-cliff-clock"></div> where you want the clock to appear.

The full embed (with MEOK credit)

<div id="meok-cliff-clock" data-style="large" data-link="https://meok.ai/industries"></div>
<script src="https://meok-attestation-api.vercel.app/clock.js"></script>

Styling options

  • data-style="small" — 0.8rem font, 1 line, no border
  • data-style="medium" — 1.2rem font, 2 lines, red border (default)
  • data-style="large" — 3rem font, 1 line, red border
  • data-link="https://..." — link the number to a URL (default: meok.ai/industries)

Why this matters
