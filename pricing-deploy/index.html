Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Pro and Enterprise are monthly. Cancel anytime, no questions. The 48h Assessment + Article 50 Kit + Watchdog Cert are one-time payments, no recurring.

Do I get a refund if I don't pass an audit?

The 48h Assessment is for self-assessment, not third-party certification. If you fail the audit (i.e. have material gaps), we deliver the remediation backlog at no extra charge. If you want third-party certification that your auditor accepts, that's the Watchdog Cert (CSOAI-issued).
