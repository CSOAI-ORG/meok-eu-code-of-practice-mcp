How it works

POST https://proofof.ai/verify
Content-Type: application/json

{"api_key": "your-key-or-empty", "tool": "audit-logger.add"}

Returns: {"allowed": true, "tier": "free", "remaining": 199, "upgrade_url": "https://proofof.ai/pricing"}

Why

Every MEOK MCP server.py is wired with a _server_meter_check() function that calls /verify on every invocation. This lets us:

  • Track usage per-tenant (for billing)
  • Cap usage at 200 free calls/day (to prevent abuse)
  • Hash-chain every call for audit trails (HMAC-signed)
  • Provide real-time stats at /scorecard

Fail-open semantics

If /verify is unreachable or KV isn't configured, _server_meter_check() returns allowed=True. The local rate-limit in _check_rate_limit() is the safety net. We never block users because our metering is down.

Why 200/day

That covers the free tier of a typical individual user (5 calls/hour for 8 hours). For teams, the $99/month plan covers 100,000 calls.
