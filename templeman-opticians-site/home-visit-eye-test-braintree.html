Domiciliary Eye Care in Braintree

A historic market town surrounded by rural villages, with care homes serving both the town and outlying communities. Templeman Opticians provides comprehensive home visit eye tests throughout Braintree, bringing professional optometry services directly to patients who are unable to visit a high-street practice.

Whether you are a care home manager looking for a reliable domiciliary optometry partner, or a family member seeking eye care for a housebound loved one, we deliver the same thorough examination you'd receive in a practice — in the comfort and familiarity of your own home.

Our optometrists are GOC registered, DBS checked, and experienced in working with patients with dementia, Parkinson's, stroke, and other conditions affecting mobility and communication.

For Housebound Patients

  • Private home visits available
  • Glasses supplied and fitted at home
