✅ NHS Home Eye Test Eligibility

Find out if you or a loved one qualifies for a free NHS-funded home eye test in Essex.

You ARE eligible if:

✓ You're a care home resident
✓ You're housebound (unable to leave home unaccompanied due to physical or mental illness/disability)
✓ You receive Pension Credit Guarantee Credit
✓ You receive Income Support or income-based JSA
✓ You're registered blind or partially sighted
✓ You have diabetes or glaucoma
✓ You're aged 60+
✓ You're under 16 (or under 19 in full-time education)

Not eligible for NHS? No problem.

Private home eye tests are available from £95-120 depending on location in Essex. Everyone deserves good eye care — whether NHS-funded or private.
