### Page 1: meok.ai/vs-vanta
- **Vanta:** $10M+ funded, SOC 2 + ISO 27001 + HIPAA audits, the "compliance copilot" for SaaS startups
- **MEOK angle:** Vanta automates *getting* the cert. MEOK *signs* the cert with Ed25519 + lets the auditor verify offline. They own the audit, we own the proof.
- **12 features:** Vanta 9/12, MEOK 12/12
- **Dual CTA:** "Try free keystone cert" (lead magnet) + "See MEOK vs Vanta full comparison" (mid-funnel)
- **Key wedge:** "Vanta says 'you're SOC 2 compliant.' MEOK says 'here's the signed proof anyone can verify without us.'"

### Page 2: meok.ai/vs-drata
- **Drata:** $100M+ funded, the "automated compliance" platform, strong in SOC 2 + ISO 27001 + HIPAA
- **MEOK angle:** Drata's auditor is YOU. MEOK's auditor is the *auditor's auditor* — the keystone's Ed25519 signature lets the auditor's auditor verify without trusting Drata OR MEOK.
- **12 features:** Drata 10/12, MEOK 12/12
- **Dual CTA:** "See keystone live demo" + "Compare Drata vs MEOK pricing"
- **Key wedge:** "Drata automates your audit. MEOK automates the *proof of your audit*."

### Page 3: meok.ai/vs-arthur-ai
- **Arthur AI:** ML model monitoring, observability, performance + bias detection
- **MEOK angle:** Arthur sees your model's behavior. MEOK signs the *evidence* of your model's compliance. Different layer, complementary stack.
- **12 features:** Arthur 7/12, MEOK 12/12 (MEOK doesn't do live monitoring; it does signed attestation of the audit results)
- **Dual CTA:** "Pair Arthur + MEOK" (joint stack) + "MEOK for AI compliance, Arthur for AI ops"
- **Key wedge:** "Arthur shows you what your model does. MEOK signs what you did about it."

### Page 4: meok.ai/vs-credo-ai

- **MEOK angle:** Credo assesses against *your* policies. MEOK signs the assessment against *the regulator's* framework. The keystone is the auditor's verify URL.

- **Dual CTA:** "Compare Credo + MEOK" + "Try MEOK's EU AI Act classifier"

### Page 5: meok.ai/vs-holistic-ai

### Page 6: meok.ai/vs-nevermined
