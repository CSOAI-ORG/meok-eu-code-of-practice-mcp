### Jersey/Guernsey Trust Structure
- **NOT Ireland holding company** — more appropriate structure is Reserved Powers Discretionary Trust in Jersey/Guernsey
- Honest benefits: IHT mitigation (APR/BPR on farm + IP), creditor protection, succession planning
- Honest non-benefits: does NOT reduce UK income tax or CGT for UK-resident settlor
- UK freehold land held via UK nominee company as bare trustee for the offshore trust
- Cost: £35,000–£65,000 setup + £5,000–£15,000/year trustee fees
- Firms: Ogier, Mourant, Carey Olsen, Viberts (Jersey)
- Full brief: `csoai-docs/meok_estate_trust_brief.md`
