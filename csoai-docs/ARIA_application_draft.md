## Problem Statement

### Problem 1: UK Families Have No Safe AI Option

Current AI assistants (Amazon Alexa, Google Home, Apple Siri, Replika) share three critical flaws:
1. **Data sovereignty:** All user data processed on US servers, subject to US surveillance law (CLOUD Act)
2. **No safety certification:** No independent body certifies that these systems are safe for vulnerable users (children, elderly, people in crisis)
3. **No transparency:** Users cannot audit what AI models are used, how data is processed, or when the system changes

UK families deserve a sovereign alternative that is British-built, locally-operated, and independently certified as safe.

### Problem 2: UK AI Companies Cannot Certify for EU Market

The EU AI Act (Regulation 2024/1689) requires high-risk AI systems to be certified by an accredited Notified Body before entering the EU market. Currently:
- All accredited Notified Bodies are EU-based (primarily German: TÜV, Dekra, BSH)
- UK companies must send sensitive AI systems and data to foreign assessors
- No UK-based body can issue EU AI Act conformity certificates
- Estimated impact: £10bn UK AI exports at risk

MEOK AI Labs.org, operating from MEOK LABS' infrastructure, fills this gap.

## Why This Addresses ARIA's Scaling Trust Arena Mission

The ARIA Scaling Trust Arena funds work that builds justified trust in advanced AI systems. MEOK.AI/MEOK AI Labs addresses this at two levels:

**Technical trust:** The MEOK AI Labs evaluation framework provides quantified, reproducible, machine-verifiable evidence that AI systems behave safely. This is not "AI ethics theatre" — it is adversarial testing that actually tries to make AI systems fail, then certifies they did not.

**Social trust:** UK families have no mechanism to verify AI safety claims. MEOK.AI makes the certification visible, legible, and revokable. If an AI agent fails a MEOK AI Labs evaluation after deployment, its certificate is revoked and it is removed from all MEOK homes automatically.

**Institutional trust:** The UK has the AI Safety Institute but no commercial certification infrastructure built on its work. MEOK AI Labs operationalises the AISI's Inspect framework into a revenue-generating, self-sustaining certification body — extending ARIA investment's impact through commercial deployment.
