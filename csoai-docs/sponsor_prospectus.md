MEOK LABS is a sovereign physical AI research institute operating at the intersection of three convergence points: distributed AI compute, agricultural robotics, and AI safety certification. We are not a startup seeking seed capital. We are a built, operational facility seeking strategic partners who want early access to the infrastructure, IP, and capabilities being assembled here.

Our model is MIT Media Lab crossed with Fraunhofer Institute: physical space, real infrastructure, open research outputs, and commercial certification services that fund the science.

## The Research Pillars

### 1. AI Safety Evaluation (MEOK AI Labs.org)

We operate the only UK-based AI safety certification body using the UK AISI Inspect framework. Our care membrane bypass evaluation tests AI systems across 7 attack categories and 16 adversarial probes — the same methodology described in UK government safety guidelines.

Services: emergency EU AI Act compliance assessments (£5k, 48hr), full certification (£75–150k), consortium membership.

Revenue target: £500k Year 1.

### 2. Physical AI + Agricultural Robotics (HARVI)

The farm is not decoration. It is a live testbed for robotic systems operating in uncontrolled outdoor environments — the hardest edge case in physical AI. HARVI (Humanoid Autonomous Robotic Validation Interface) is our internal robotics evaluation protocol.

Partners get: access to the testbed, co-authorship on robotic safety papers, participation in MoD/Dstl agricultural autonomy programmes.

### 3. Sovereign AI Infrastructure

MEOK.AI is a distributed home AI operating system — local inference, no cloud dependency, 15 model providers, Byzantine fault-tolerant agent governance. It is the consumer layer above MEOK AI Labs's enterprise safety engine.

It is also the most important proof point: we have built a production AI system (22 APIs, 15 pages, 307 tests passing) and run it through our own safety evaluation framework. We eat our own cooking.
