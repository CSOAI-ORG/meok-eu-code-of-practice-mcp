# CSOAI vs CAI — Competitive Differentiation Matrix (16 Jun 2026)

## TL;DR

**CSOAI's `asisecurity.ai` and CAI (Cybersecurity AI) are NOT direct competitors.** They occupy different layers of the AI security stack. CAI protects systems from hackers (general cybersecurity); CSOAI protects AI systems from misuse, bias, and regulatory non-compliance (AI-specific compliance). Both can coexist — or CSOAI can absorb CAI's approach into a broader compliance offering.

**The combined offering = "AI system security + AI regulatory compliance" = a moat no single competitor has.**
