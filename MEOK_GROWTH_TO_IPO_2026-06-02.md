Investors back a sentence, not a portfolio. Three candidates, ranked:
1. **"Compliance infrastructure for the agentic web."** As agents/robots cross regions, someone has
   to keep them legal + audited. CSOAI charter (the standard) + MEOK LAW (the product) + the crosswalks
   + signed audit. Timely (EU AI Act era), B2B budgets, defensible moat. **← strongest.**
2. **"The governance + memory layer every AI agent plugs into."** The MCP catalogue + SOV3 as the
   safe substrate. Developer-led, big TAM, but crowded.
3. **"The AI you own."** MEOK ONE consumer sovereignty play. Great story, hardest monetisation.

Recommendation: **lead B2B with #1, let MEOK ONE (#3) be the consumer proof + funnel.** One deck, one wedge.

- **Distribution flywheel:** 218 MCPs in the registries → developers discover → land on the platform
  → convert to MEOK LAW / Pro. Owned, compounding, cheap.
- **Audited governance:** SIGIL hash-chained audit + the BFT council is genuinely differentiated as
  agents get more autonomous + more regulated.
