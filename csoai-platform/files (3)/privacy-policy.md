## 8. YOUR RIGHTS

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

### 8.1 Access
Request a copy of your personal data.

### 8.2 Rectification
Request correction of inaccurate data.

### 8.3 Erasure
Request deletion ("right to be forgotten") where:
- Data no longer necessary
- You withdraw consent
- You object to processing
- Data unlawfully processed

### 8.4 Restriction
Request limited processing in certain circumstances.

### 8.5 Data Portability
Receive your data in machine-readable format.

### 8.6 Objection
Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.

### 8.7 Automated Decision-Making
Not be subject to solely automated decisions with significant effects.

### 8.8 Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent at any time (where processing based on consent).

**To exercise your rights:** Email privacy@csoai.org

We respond to requests within one month. Complex requests may take up to three months.
