## AGREEMENT

### 1. DEFINITIONS

**1.1** "AI System" has the meaning set forth in Schedule A of the Charter.

**1.2** "Charter" means the CSOAI Partnership Charter, including all Schedules, as amended from time to time in accordance with Article 15 thereof.

**1.3** "License" means the authorization granted under this Agreement to develop and/or deploy AI Systems.

**1.4** "License Tier" means the tier classification assigned to Licensee pursuant to Schedule E of the Charter.

**1.5** "Prosperity Contribution" means the mandatory contribution to the AI Prosperity Fund calculated pursuant to Schedule F of the Charter.

**1.6** "License Year" means each 12-month period commencing on the Effective Date or any anniversary thereof.

---

### 2. GRANT OF LICENSE

**2.1 License Grant.** Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, CSOAI grants to Licensee a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to develop and/or deploy AI Systems in accordance with the Charter.

**2.2 Scope.** The License permits Licensee to:
(a) Develop AI Systems in compliance with Charter requirements;
(b) Deploy AI Systems in compliance with Charter requirements;
(c) Use CSOAI trademarks to indicate licensed status;
(d) Access CSOAI resources and support services.

**2.3 Limitations.** The License does not permit Licensee to:
(a) Develop or deploy Level 4 (Prohibited) AI Systems as defined in Schedule B;
(b) Transfer or sublicense rights under this Agreement;
(c) Represent itself as CSOAI or an agent of CSOAI;
(d) Use the License for any purpose not contemplated by the Charter.

**2.4 License Tier.** Licensee is initially classified as Tier [X] based on declared AI-Related Revenue of [AMOUNT]. Tier classification may be adjusted pursuant to Section 6.

---

### 3. ACCEPTANCE OF CHARTER

**3.1 Incorporation by Reference.** The Charter is incorporated by reference into this Agreement. Licensee agrees to be bound by all applicable provisions of the Charter.

**3.2 Amendments.** Licensee acknowledges that the Charter may be amended in accordance with Article 15 thereof. Amendments shall be binding on Licensee upon the effective date specified in the amendment, provided CSOAI has given Licensee at least 60 days' notice of material amendments.

**3.3 Hierarchy.** In the event of conflict between this Agreement and the Charter, the Charter shall prevail, except with respect to provisions relating solely to the contractual relationship between the Parties.

---

### 4. FEES AND CONTRIBUTIONS

**4.1 Annual License Fee.** Licensee shall pay to CSOAI an annual license fee as specified in Schedule E of the Charter for Licensee's License Tier:

| Tier | Annual Fee |
|------|------------|
| Tier 1 | $10,000,000 |
| Tier 2 | $1,000,000 |
| Tier 3 | $100,000 |
| Tier 4 | $25,000 |
| Tier 5 | $1,000 |

**4.2 Prosperity Contribution.** In addition to the Annual License Fee, Licensee shall make Prosperity Contributions to the AI Prosperity Fund as specified in the Charter:

| Tier | Contribution Rate |
|------|-------------------|
| Tier 1 | 5% of AI-Related Profit |
| Tier 2 | 4% of AI-Related Profit |
| Tier 3 | 3% of AI-Related Profit |
| Tier 4 | 2% of AI-Related Profit |
| Tier 5 | 1% of AI-Related Profit |

**4.3 Calculation.** AI-Related Profit shall be calculated in accordance with Schedule F of the Charter. Licensee shall provide CSOAI with the information necessary to verify calculations.

**4.4 Payment Terms.**
(a) Annual License Fee: Due within 30 days of invoice, payable annually in advance.
(b) Prosperity Contribution: Quarterly estimated payments due 15 days after quarter end; annual reconciliation within 90 days of Licensee's fiscal year end.

**4.5 Late Payment.** Amounts not paid when due shall bear interest at the rate of [Bank of England base rate + 3%] per annum, calculated from the due date until payment.

**4.6 Taxes.** Fees and Contributions are exclusive of any applicable taxes, which shall be Licensee's responsibility.

---

### 5. LICENSEE OBLIGATIONS

**5.1 Compliance.** Licensee shall comply with all applicable provisions of the Charter, including but not limited to:
(a) Technical standards (Part III);
(b) Sector-specific requirements (Part IV) applicable to Licensee's activities;
(c) Reporting requirements (Article 39);
(d) Record-keeping requirements (Article 40);
(e) Incident response requirements (Article 41).

**5.2 Personnel.** Licensee shall maintain CEASAI-certified personnel as required by the Charter for Licensee's License Tier:
(a) Tier 1-2: Chief AI Safety Officer (Expert) plus required Professionals;
(b) Tier 3-4: Designated AI Safety Lead (Professional);
(c) Tier 5: Designated Responsible Person (Associate).

**5.3 Audits.** Licensee shall:
(a) Conduct self-assessments as required by Schedule K;
(b) Submit to third-party audits at the frequency specified for Licensee's Tier;
(c) Cooperate with CSOAI audits conducted pursuant to Article 38;
(d) Provide auditors with access to relevant personnel, systems, and records;
(e) Respond to audit findings within specified timeframes.

**5.4 Reporting.** Licensee shall submit to CSOAI:
(a) Quarterly reports as specified in Article 39.2.1;
(b) Annual reports as specified in Article 39.2.2;
(c) Incident reports as specified in Article 39.3.1;
(d) Change reports as specified in Article 39.3.2.

**5.5 Cooperation.** Licensee shall cooperate with:
(a) CSOAI investigations;
(b) Byzantine Council monitoring;
(c) Human Council inquiries;
(d) Public Watchdog requests (as applicable).

**5.6 Insurance.** Licensee shall maintain appropriate insurance coverage for AI-related liabilities, at levels commensurate with Licensee's activities and risk profile.

---

### 6. TIER ADJUSTMENT

**6.1 Annual Review.** Licensee's Tier classification shall be reviewed annually based on AI-Related Revenue for the preceding fiscal year.

**6.2 Tier Increase.** If Licensee's AI-Related Revenue increases to a level warranting a higher Tier, Licensee shall:
(a) Notify CSOAI within 30 days;
(b) Begin paying fees at the higher Tier rate from the next License Year;
(c) Meet higher Tier requirements within 180 days.

**6.3 Tier Decrease.** Licensee may request Tier decrease if AI-Related Revenue decreases. CSOAI shall review and approve or deny within 60 days.

---

### 7. TERM AND TERMINATION

**7.1 Term.** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial term of one (1) year ("Initial Term"), automatically renewing for successive one-year periods ("Renewal Terms") unless terminated in accordance with this Section.

**7.2 Termination for Convenience.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement at the end of any License Year by giving at least 90 days' prior written notice.

**7.3 Termination for Cause.** CSOAI may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if:
(a) Licensee commits a material breach and fails to cure within 30 days of notice;
(b) Licensee commits a Tier 3 or Tier 4 violation under Article 17;
(c) Licensee's License is revoked pursuant to Article 17;
(d) Licensee becomes insolvent or enters bankruptcy proceedings.

**7.4 Effect of Termination.** Upon termination:
(a) The License granted hereunder shall immediately terminate;
(b) Licensee shall cease all activities requiring the License;
(c) Licensee shall cease use of CSOAI trademarks;
(d) Licensee shall pay all outstanding fees and Contributions;
(e) Provisions that by their nature should survive shall survive.

**7.5 Wind-Down Period.** Upon termination, Licensee shall have 90 days to wind down AI operations or transfer them to a licensed operator, unless termination was for a Tier 4 violation requiring immediate cessation.

**8.2 Enforcement Actions.** Licensee acknowledges that CSOAI may take enforcement actions pursuant to Article 17 of the Charter, including but not limited to:
(a) Warnings;
(b) Probation;
(c) Fines;
(d) License suspension;
(e) License revocation.

**8.3 Due Process.** Licensee shall be afforded due process as specified in Article 17 and Schedule J, including notice, opportunity to respond, and appeal rights.

**8.4 Fines.** Fines imposed pursuant to Article 17 shall be paid to CSOAI within 30 days of final determination (after exhaustion of appeals, if any).

### 11. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

**11.1 Exclusion of Consequential Damages.** NEITHER PARTY SHALL BE LIABLE FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES ARISING OUT OF THIS AGREEMENT.

**11.2 Cap on Liability.** EXCEPT FOR BREACHES OF CONFIDENTIALITY, GROSS NEGLIGENCE, OR WILLFUL MISCONDUCT, EACH PARTY'S TOTAL LIABILITY UNDER THIS AGREEMENT SHALL NOT EXCEED THE FEES PAID BY LICENSEE IN THE 12 MONTHS PRECEDING THE CLAIM.

**11.3 No Limitation for Certain Claims.** Nothing in this Agreement limits liability for: (a) death or personal injury caused by negligence; (b) fraud; (c) any liability that cannot be limited by law.

### 13. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

**13.1 Negotiation.** The Parties shall attempt to resolve disputes through good-faith negotiation.

**13.2 Mediation.** If negotiation fails, the Parties shall attempt mediation before a mediator agreed by the Parties or appointed by the Centre for Effective Dispute Resolution (CEDR).

**13.3 Arbitration.** If mediation fails, disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration:
(a) Under the LCIA Arbitration Rules;
(b) Seat of arbitration: London;
(c) Language: English;
(d) One arbitrator for disputes under £1,000,000; three arbitrators otherwise.

**13.4 Interim Relief.** Nothing prevents either Party from seeking interim relief from courts of competent jurisdiction.
