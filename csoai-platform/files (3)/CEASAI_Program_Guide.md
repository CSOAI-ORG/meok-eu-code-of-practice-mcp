## PROGRAM OVERVIEW

CEASAI (Certified Engineer for AI Safety and Implementation) is the professional certification program established by CSOAI to ensure competent oversight of AI systems. CEASAI certification demonstrates that an individual possesses the knowledge, skills, and ethical commitment required for responsible AI development and deployment.

### Why CEASAI Matters

- **Regulatory Requirement:** CSOAI-licensed organizations must employ CEASAI-certified personnel
- **Professional Credential:** Industry-recognized certification for AI safety professionals
- **Ethical Commitment:** Certified professionals commit to the CSOAI Code of Ethics
- **Career Advancement:** Demonstrates expertise in rapidly growing field
- **Global Recognition:** Valid across all CSOAI-participating jurisdictions

## CERTIFICATION LEVELS

### Level 1: CEASAI Associate (CEASAI-A)

**Target Audience:** 
- Professionals beginning AI safety careers
- Technical staff working with AI systems
- Managers overseeing AI projects
- Compliance officers

**Prerequisites:** None (entry level)

**Requirements:**
- Complete Foundation Course (40 hours)
- Pass Associate Examination (minimum 70%)
- Sign Code of Ethics commitment
- Pay certification fee ($250)

**Validity:** 2 years, renewable with continuing education

**Organizational Requirement:** Required for operational roles in Tier 4-5 licensed organizations

---

### Level 2: CEASAI Professional (CEASAI-P)

**Target Audience:**
- Practicing AI safety engineers
- AI project leads
- Compliance managers
- Risk assessment specialists

**Prerequisites:**
- CEASAI Associate for 2+ years, OR
- 4+ years relevant professional experience with portfolio review

**Requirements:**
- Complete Advanced Modules (80 hours total)
- Pass Professional Examination (written 50% + case study 50%)
- Demonstrate practical experience (portfolio submission)
- Sign Code of Ethics commitment
- Pay certification fee ($500)

**Validity:** 3 years, renewable with continuing education

**Organizational Requirement:** Required for AI safety lead roles in Tier 3-4 licensed organizations

---

### Level 3: CEASAI Expert (CEASAI-E)

**Target Audience:**
- Senior AI safety leaders
- Chief AI Safety Officers
- Policy advisors
- Academic researchers

**Prerequisites:**
- CEASAI Professional for 3+ years
- Demonstrated leadership in AI safety

**Requirements:**
- Complete Expert Modules (40 hours)
- Pass Expert Examination (oral + portfolio review)
- Published contribution to field (paper, standard, tool)
- Teaching or mentoring experience
- Sign Code of Ethics commitment
- Pay certification fee ($1,000)

**Validity:** 5 years, renewable with continuing education + contribution

**Organizational Requirement:** Required for Chief AI Safety Officer role in Tier 1-2 licensed organizations

## EXAMINATIONS

### Associate Examination
- **Format:** 100 multiple-choice questions
- **Duration:** 2 hours
- **Passing Score:** 70% (70 correct)
- **Topics:** All Foundation Course modules
- **Delivery:** Online proctored or testing center

**Sample Questions:**
1. According to the CSOAI Charter, which article establishes the Byzantine Council?
   a) Article 10
   b) Article 11 ✓
   c) Article 12
   d) Article 13

2. What is the minimum number of AI systems required for Byzantine Council operational quorum?
   a) 3
   b) 5
   c) 7 ✓
   d) 11

3. Under Schedule B, which risk level includes systems that are prohibited?
   a) Level 1
   b) Level 2
   c) Level 3
   d) Level 4 ✓

### Professional Examination

**Part A: Written (50%)**
- 50 multiple-choice questions (1 hour)
- 5 short-answer questions (1 hour)
- Topics: Advanced modules + application

**Part B: Case Study (50%)**
- Real-world scenario provided
- 2-hour written analysis
- Evaluate: risk assessment, mitigation strategy, compliance approach
- Graded by certified examiners

### Expert Examination

**Part A: Oral Examination (50%)**
- 1-hour panel interview
- 3 certified Expert examiners
- Defense of portfolio
- Policy and leadership questions

**Part B: Portfolio Review (50%)**
- Career history and achievements
- Published contributions
- Leadership examples
- Teaching/mentoring evidence

## CODE OF ETHICS

All CEASAI certified professionals commit to:

### 1. Human Welfare First
I will prioritize human welfare above commercial interests, organizational pressure, or personal advancement in all AI-related decisions.

### 2. Honesty and Transparency
I will be truthful about AI capabilities, limitations, and risks. I will not misrepresent AI systems to users, stakeholders, or regulators.

### 3. Competence and Diligence
I will maintain current knowledge in AI safety, apply rigorous standards, and acknowledge the limits of my expertise.

### 4. Ethical Conduct
I will adhere to the ethical principles in the CSOAI Charter and refuse to participate in AI applications that violate human dignity or rights.

### 5. Reporting Obligation
I will report safety concerns through appropriate channels, including the Public Watchdog when internal channels are ineffective.

### 6. Continuous Learning
I will maintain my competence through continuing education and stay current with evolving best practices.

### 7. Professional Integrity
I will not engage in deception, misrepresentation, or conflicts of interest that could compromise AI safety.

**Violation of this Code may result in suspension or revocation of certification.**

## APPLICATION PROCESS

### Step 1: Eligibility Verification
- Review prerequisites for target level
- Gather required documentation
- Confirm experience requirements (if applicable)

### Step 2: Training Completion
- Complete required courses
- Obtain course completion certificates
- Meet attendance requirements (80% minimum)

### Step 3: Examination Registration
- Create CEASAI portal account
- Submit application with documentation
- Pay examination fee
- Schedule examination date

### Step 4: Examination
- Complete examination per requirements
- Await results (2-4 weeks)
- Receive preliminary result

### Step 5: Certification
- Upon passing, receive digital certificate
- Added to public CEASAI registry
- Receive certification materials
- Begin continuing education tracking
