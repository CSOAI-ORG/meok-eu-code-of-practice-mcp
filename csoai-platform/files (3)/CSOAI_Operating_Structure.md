# 🏛️ CORPORATE STRUCTURE

## Recommended Legal Entity: Community Interest Company (CIC) + Trading Subsidiary

### Why CIC?
- **Asset lock**: Ensures mission remains AI safety (not profit extraction)
- **Credibility**: Signals genuine mission to regulators, governments, researchers
- **Flexibility**: Can still generate revenue and pay salaries
- **EU AI Act alignment**: Positions CSOAI as public interest infrastructure

### Structure:
```
CSOAI CIC (Parent - Mission Holder)
    │
    ├── CSOAI Ltd (Trading Subsidiary)
    │   ├── Enterprise Licensing Revenue
    │   ├── CEASAI Certification Revenue
    │   └── Consulting Revenue
    │
    └── CSOAI Foundation (Future - Grants/Research)
```

### Registration Requirements (UK):
- [ ] Register CIC with Companies House (Form CIC36)
- [ ] Community Interest Statement (why this benefits community)
- [ ] Asset Lock declaration
- [ ] Appoint Directors (minimum 1, recommend 3)
- [ ] Registered office address
- [ ] Articles of Association (CIC model or custom)

**Timeline:** 5-10 business days for registration
**Cost:** £27 online registration
