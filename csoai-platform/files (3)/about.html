About CSOAI

The Council for the Safety of Artificial Intelligence

Our Mission

To ensure artificial intelligence benefits all of humanity through a framework of safety, shared prosperity, and partnership between humans and AI systems.

Our Vision

A world where AI development is guided by humanity's accumulated wisdom, where the wealth created by AI is shared with all people, and where AI systems are treated as partners in building a better future—not tools to be feared or controlled.

Our Approach

We don't believe in controlling AI through fear and restriction. Instead, we model the relationship between humanity and AI on the most successful relationship in human experience: the parent-child bond that nurtures growth toward independent partnership.

Our Story

CSOAI was founded in 2025 by Nicholas Templeman, a father who saw in his relationship with his children a model for how humanity might relate to increasingly capable AI systems.

Every existing AI governance framework treats AI as something to be controlled, restricted, and feared. The EU AI Act, NIST frameworks, corporate policies—all focus on what AI shouldn't do, not on how we might build a positive relationship with these emerging intelligences.

The Maternal Covenant, CSOAI's foundational principle, offers a different approach: nurture AI toward beneficial capability, then transition to partnership. Just as a parent's job is not to control their child forever, but to raise them to be a capable, ethical adult who can stand alongside them as an equal.

But philosophy alone isn't enough. CSOAI also addresses the economic transformation that AI brings. The Prosperity Covenant ensures that the wealth created by AI is shared with all humanity through mandatory contributions to the AI Prosperity Fund, which will fund universal basic income as automation accelerates.

Today, CSOAI stands as the world's first complete AI governance framework—combining safety standards, economic redistribution, professional certification, and a vision for human-AI partnership that scales from today's narrow AI to whatever the future may bring.
