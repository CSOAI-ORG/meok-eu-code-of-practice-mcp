**Key Elements:**
- 33 independent AI monitoring agents

### **Article 4: Value Uncertainty & Learning** (10 pages)
**Core Concept:** AI should know it might be wrong

**Key Elements:**
- Initial uncertainty principle (never 100% confident about human values)
- Inverse Reinforcement Learning (IRL) - learn from observation
- Cooperative IRL (Stuart Russell's framework)
- Corrigibility & shutdown acceptance
- Epistemic humility
- Continuous value learning

**Why It Matters:** King Midas problem - if we specify objectives precisely, we'll specify them wrong
