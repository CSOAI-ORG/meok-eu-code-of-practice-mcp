## 2. MEMBERSHIP TIERS

### 2.1 Tier Definitions

| Tier | Annual Revenue | Annual Fee |
|------|---------------|------------|
| Startup | <£1M | Free (Year 1) |

- Founding Council voting rights
- Charter input privileges
- Recognition in perpetuity

---

## 3. MEMBER OBLIGATIONS

### 3.1 The Member agrees to:

a) Comply with the CSOAI Partnership Charter (52 articles)

b) Submit to compliance assessments as required by risk tier

c) Report AI safety incidents within required timeframes:
   - Critical: 1 hour
   - High: 24 hours
   - Medium: 7 days
   - Low: 30 days

d) Maintain accurate records of AI systems under CSOAI governance

e) Pay membership fees when due

f) Participate in good faith in CSOAI governance processes

### 3.2 The Member shall NOT:

a) Misrepresent CSOAI certification or membership status

b) Use CSOAI branding without authorization

c) Engage in activities that bring CSOAI into disrepute

d) Share confidential CSOAI materials with non-members

---

## 4. CSOAI OBLIGATIONS

### 4.1 CSOAI agrees to:

a) Provide access to compliance tools and frameworks

b) Conduct fair and transparent assessments

c) Maintain confidentiality of Member information

d) Provide reasonable notice of Charter amendments

e) Operate governance processes democratically

f) Maintain the Byzantine Council monitoring infrastructure

---

## 5. LICENSING & CERTIFICATION

### 5.1 License Grant
Upon successful compliance assessment, CSOAI grants Member a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to:
- Display CSOAI certification marks
- Reference CSOAI compliance status
- Access CSOAI member resources

### 5.2 License Conditions
License remains valid subject to:
- Continued compliance with Charter
- Payment of fees
- Satisfactory audit results

### 5.3 License Revocation
CSOAI may revoke license for:
- Material non-compliance
- Safety incidents with inadequate response
- Non-payment (after 60-day cure period)
- Misuse of CSOAI certification marks

---

## 6. FEES & PAYMENT

### 6.1 Payment Terms
- Annual fees due within 30 days of invoice
- Late payment: 2% monthly interest
- Non-payment after 90 days: Membership suspension

### 6.2 Refunds
- Pro-rata refund if CSOAI terminates without cause
- No refund for Member-initiated termination
- No refund for termination due to Member breach

---

## 7. CONFIDENTIALITY

### 7.1 Confidential Information
Both parties agree to protect:
- Assessment results (unless public disclosure required)
- Proprietary business information
- Technical architecture details
- Member lists (except publicly acknowledged members)

### 7.2 Exceptions
Confidentiality does not apply to:
- Publicly available information
- Information required by law to disclose
- Safety incidents requiring public notification

---

## 8. LIABILITY

### 8.1 Limitation of Liability
CSOAI's total liability shall not exceed fees paid in the preceding 12 months.

### 8.2 Exclusions
CSOAI is not liable for:
- Indirect or consequential damages
- Loss of profits
- Third-party claims against Member
- Regulatory actions against Member

### 8.3 Member Indemnification
Member indemnifies CSOAI against claims arising from:
- Member's AI systems
- Member's misrepresentation of CSOAI status
- Member's breach of this Agreement

---

## 9. TERM & TERMINATION

### 9.1 Term
- Initial term: 12 months
- Auto-renewal unless 60-day notice given

### 9.2 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate immediately for:
- Material breach (uncured after 30 days notice)
- Insolvency
- Illegal activity

### 9.3 Effect of Termination
Upon termination:
- License to use CSOAI marks ceases immediately
- Member must remove all CSOAI branding within 30 days
- Confidentiality obligations survive for 5 years
