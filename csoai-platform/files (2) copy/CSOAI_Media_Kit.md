## Long (200 words)
CSOAI (Council for the Safety of Artificial Intelligence) provides the world's first comprehensive AI governance framework designed to ensure artificial intelligence benefits all of humanity.

The CSOAI Partnership Charter is a 52-article document establishing safety standards, licensing requirements, professional certification, and economic redistribution mechanisms. Key innovations include:

- **Prosperity Contributions:** Licensed organizations contribute 1-5% of AI profits to the AI Prosperity Fund, which automatically distributes Universal Basic Income when thresholds are reached.

- **Byzantine Council:** Real-time AI-to-AI safety monitoring using fault-tolerant consensus—the first operational system of its kind.

- **CEASAI Certification:** Professional credentials for AI safety practitioners at Associate, Professional, and Expert levels.

- **Wisdom Traditions Integration:** Governance grounded in humanity's accumulated ethical wisdom across philosophical and religious traditions.

The Charter exceeds EU AI Act requirements while addressing economic displacement that other frameworks ignore. CSOAI pursues official recognition as an approved conformity assessment body in multiple jurisdictions.

Founded by Nicholas Templeman and supported by a Founding Council of industry executives, policy experts, and AI safety researchers, CSOAI is headquartered in the United Kingdom with global operations beginning January 2026.

For more information, visit csoai.org.
