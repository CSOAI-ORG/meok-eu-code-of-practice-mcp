## 1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites function properly, remember your preferences, and provide analytics.

---

## 2. HOW WE USE COOKIES

### 2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function. You cannot opt out of these.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| session_id | Maintains your login session | Session |
| csrf_token | Security - prevents cross-site attacks | Session |
| cookie_consent | Remembers your cookie preferences | 1 year |

### 2.2 Functional Cookies

These remember your choices and preferences.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| language | Remembers language preference | 1 year |
| timezone | Remembers timezone setting | 1 year |
| theme | Remembers display preferences | 1 year |

### 2.3 Analytics Cookies

These help us understand how visitors use our website.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| _ga | Google Analytics - distinguishes users | 2 years |
| _gid | Google Analytics - distinguishes users | 24 hours |
| _gat | Google Analytics - throttles requests | 1 minute |

### 2.4 Marketing Cookies

We currently do not use marketing or advertising cookies.

## 4. MANAGING COOKIES

### 4.1 Cookie Banner

When you first visit our site, you can choose which cookies to accept via our cookie banner.

### 4.2 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser:

- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
- **Firefox:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
- **Edge:** Settings → Privacy → Cookies

### 4.3 Opt-Out Links

- Google Analytics: tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

---

## 5. IMPACT OF DISABLING COOKIES

If you disable cookies:
- Essential site functions may not work
- You may need to re-enter preferences each visit
- Some features may be unavailable
- We cannot analyze and improve your experience
