**Date:** January 12, 2026

## OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

This document summarizes the revisions made to CSOAI Charter Articles 8 and 17 to include comprehensive Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for Mandatory Prosperity Contributions.

## ARTICLE 8 REVISIONS

### New Section 8.3: Standard Operating Procedures for Major Companies

Six new SOPs have been added:

| SOP Number | Title | Purpose |
|------------|-------|---------|
| SOP-MPC-001 | Initial Registration and Classification | Standardized procedures for company registration |
| SOP-MPC-002 | Quarterly Contribution Calculation | 30-day process for calculating and paying contributions |
| SOP-MPC-003 | Annual Reconciliation and Audit | 90-day reconciliation and independent audit procedures |
| SOP-MPC-004 | Compliance Monitoring and Reporting | Continuous monitoring and escalation procedures |
| SOP-MPC-005 | Dispute Resolution for Contribution Calculations | Four-step dispute resolution process |
| SOP-MPC-006 | Change in Tier Status | Procedures for tier reclassification |

Three standardized language templates have been added:

1. **Mandatory Prosperity Contribution Acknowledgment** - Required contractual provision
2. **Board Resolution Language** - Standard board resolution for compliance
3. **Annual Report Disclosure** - Required disclosure for public companies

21 new forms standardize the contribution process:
- Registration forms (MPC-REG-001 through MPC-PAY-001)

## ARTICLE 17 REVISIONS

### New Section 17.3: Mandatory Prosperity Contribution Enforcement

Five new enforcement SOPs have been added:

| SOP Number | Title | Purpose |
|------------|-------|---------|
| SOP-ENF-MPC-001 | Late Payment Enforcement | Graduated enforcement timeline (7-91+ days) |
| SOP-ENF-MPC-002 | Underpayment Investigation | 90-day investigation procedure |
| SOP-ENF-MPC-003 | Penalty Calculation | Standardized penalty formula |
| SOP-ENF-MPC-004 | Fraud Investigation and Referral | Fraud investigation and criminal referral |
| SOP-ENF-MPC-005 | License Sanctions for Contribution Violations | Five-level sanction framework |

### Penalty Calculation Formula

```
Total Penalty = (Underpayment Amount × Severity Multiplier × Company Size Factor × Culpability Factor) + Late Payment Surcharges + Investigation Costs
```

**Severity Multipliers:**
- <5%: 1.0x (cure period only)
- 5-10%: 2.0x
- 10-15%: 3.0x
- 15-20%: 4.0x
- >20%: 5.0x

**Company Size Factors:**
- Tier 1 (>$10B): 10x
- Tier 2 ($1B-$10B): 5x
- Tier 3 ($100M-$1B): 2x
- Tier 4 ($10M-$100M): 1x
- Tier 5 (<$10M): 0.5x

**Culpability Factors:**
- Inadvertent: 1.0x
- Negligent: 1.5x
- Reckless: 2.0x
- Knowing: 3.0x
- Intentional: 5.0x

### Five-Level Sanction Framework

| Level | Sanction | Trigger | Duration |
|-------|----------|---------|----------|
| 1 | Enhanced Monitoring | First moderate violation | 6-12 months |
| 2 | Probation | Serious/repeated violations | 12-24 months |
| 3 | Operational Restrictions | Severe violations | Until compliance |
| 4 | Suspension | Catastrophic violations | Min. 90 days |
| 5 | Revocation | Fraud/repeated catastrophic | Permanent (min. 5 years) |

### Key Enforcement Forms Introduced

20 new enforcement forms standardize the process:
- Late payment forms (ENF-MPC-001 through ENF-MPC-005)
- Investigation forms (ENF-INV-001 through ENF-INV-005)
- Fraud forms (ENF-FRD-001 through ENF-FRD-004)
- License sanction forms (ENF-LIC-001 through ENF-LIC-005)

## COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS FOR MAJOR COMPANIES

Companies in Tiers 1-3 (revenue >$100M) must:

1. **Register** within 30 days of threshold using Form MPC-REG-001
2. **Designate** Compliance Officer and CFO as primary contacts
3. **Calculate** quarterly contributions using Form MPC-CALC-001
4. **Submit** quarterly reports by 30th of following month
5. **Complete** annual reconciliation and audit within 90 days of fiscal year-end
6. **Maintain** compliance dashboard access
7. **Report** material changes within 30 days
