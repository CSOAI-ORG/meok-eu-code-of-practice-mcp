## The Problem

**Section Title:**
Why CSOAI Exists

**Body:**
AI is creating unprecedented wealth while threatening unprecedented displacement. Existing governance frameworks address safety but ignore economic justice.

- The EU AI Act has no wealth redistribution mechanism
- Corporate AI policies are voluntary and self-serving
- OECD principles are non-binding soft law
- No framework combines safety AND economic justice

Someone had to build it. So we did.

---

## Our Approach

**Section Title:**
Partnership, Not Control

**Body:**
We call it the Maternal Covenant.

Most AI governance treats artificial intelligence as something to fear, restrict, and control. We take a different approach.

A good parent nurtures, protects, and gradually releases control as their child matures. That's exactly how CSOAI approaches AI governance.

Not fear. Not restriction. Partnership.

As AI systems demonstrate alignment with human values, they earn greater autonomy. The relationship evolves. Trust is built. Partnership emerges.

This isn't naive optimism. It's structured progression with safety guarantees at every stage.
