Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as "AI-related revenue"?

Revenue from products or services that incorporate AI as a significant component. See Schedule F of the Charter for detailed calculation methods.

What if I'm not sure which tier I belong to?

Start with your best estimate. We'll verify during the application process and adjust if needed. You can always tier up as you grow.

Where does my prosperity contribution go?

To the AI Prosperity Fund, a separate charitable trust. When thresholds are reached, it funds triggered universal basic income programs.

Is the license mandatory?

Currently voluntary. However, expect market pressure as clients, partners, and insurers increasingly require CSOAI certification.

How does this relate to EU AI Act compliance?

CSOAI license requirements map directly to EU AI Act articles. Licensed organizations can demonstrate regulatory alignment through Charter compliance.
