Curriculum Overview

Foundation Course (40 Hours)

Module 1: AI Systems

Fundamentals, architectures, capabilities, limitations

Module 2: AI Safety

Alignment, technical safety, operational safety

Module 3: CSOAI Charter

Principles, governance, standards

Module 4: Ethics

Frameworks, wisdom traditions, decision-making

Module 5: Implementation

Risk assessment, compliance, incident response

Advanced Modules (80 Hours)

Module 6: Technical Safety

Architecture, testing, monitoring, red teaming

Module 7: Risk Management

Quantitative assessment, sector-specific factors

Module 8: Governance

EU AI Act, NIST, ISO, organizational governance

Module 9: Sectors

Healthcare, finance, transportation, defense, etc.

Expert Modules (40 Hours)

Module 10: Leadership

Strategy, change management, crisis leadership

Module 11: Advanced Topics

Frontier AI, policy, research, future scenarios
