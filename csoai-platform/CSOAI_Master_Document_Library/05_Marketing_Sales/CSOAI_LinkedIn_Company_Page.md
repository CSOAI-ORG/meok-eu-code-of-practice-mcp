CSOAI (Council for the Safety of Artificial Intelligence) provides the world's first comprehensive AI governance infrastructure combining safety standards, economic justice, and human-AI partnership.

No other framework combines what we offer:

• Mandatory Prosperity Contributions → Universal Basic Income
• Byzantine Council AI-to-AI real-time safety monitoring  
• Wisdom traditions integrated into technical governance
• Complete licensing + enforcement + certification system

**Our Mission**

To ensure artificial intelligence benefits all of humanity, not just the few. We believe AI prosperity should be universal.

**What We Provide**

📋 LICENSING
Tiered licensing framework for AI developers and deployers, with compliance support and audit services.

🎓 CEASAI CERTIFICATION  
Professional credentials for AI safety practitioners. Three levels: Associate, Professional, Expert.

Transparency infrastructure including license verification, incident tracking, and public participation.

💰 PROSPERITY FUND
Mandatory contributions from licensed organizations flow to AI Prosperity Fund, distributing UBI when thresholds are reached.
