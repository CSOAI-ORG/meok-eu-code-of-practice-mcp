Council Responsibilities

**Governance:**

**Charter Stewardship:**
Members review and ratify Charter amendments, ensuring the framework evolves appropriately as AI technology and regulation develop.

**Advocacy:**
Council members serve as ambassadors for CSOAI's mission, engaging with industry, government, and public stakeholders.

## FAQ Section

**How are Council members selected?**
Applications are reviewed by the Executive Director and existing Council members. We seek diversity of expertise, geography, and perspective.

**Is Council membership paid?**
Founding Council membership is voluntary and unpaid. Members contribute their time and expertise to advance the mission.

**What is the time commitment?**
Quarterly meetings (2-3 hours each) plus occasional review of documents and participation in working groups as available.

**How long is the term?**
Founding Council members serve until the Council transitions to elected governance per the Charter (anticipated 2028).

**Can I remain anonymous?**
Council members are publicly listed. If you wish to contribute privately, contact us about advisory roles.
