# CSOAI Master Document Library Index

**CSOAI launches January 2026 as an AI safety certification body** requiring comprehensive documentation across governance, compliance, operations, and commercial functions. This index provides every document needed to build an investor-ready, regulator-ready organization—organized by priority with implementation guidance.

The documentation burden is substantial but achievable. Based on analysis of ISO certification body requirements, EU AI Act Notified Body mandates, and successful certification organizations like IAPP and BSI, CSOAI requires approximately **180+ distinct documents** across nine functional areas. Essential launch documents number roughly 75, with the remainder developed over the first 24 months.

---

## Priority classification system

Documents are classified into four tiers:

- **P1 (Critical)**: Required before January 2026 launch—legal necessity or operational prerequisite
- **P2 (High)**: Required within first 6 months—accreditation dependencies or revenue generation
- **P3 (Important)**: Required within first 12 months—scalability and compliance maturation  
- **P4 (Developmental)**: Year 2+ development—optimization and expansion
