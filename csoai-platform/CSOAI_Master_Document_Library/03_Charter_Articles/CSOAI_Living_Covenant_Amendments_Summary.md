# SUMMARY OF CHANGES

| Before | After | Why |

**Impact:** All references throughout document updated. Signals evolution from regulatory document to sacred partnership framework.

## 2. NEW PREAMBLE: The Great Work

**Added:** Complete new preamble framing CSOAI's mission as "The Great Work" - the alchemical transformation of raw technological capability into wisdom-infused systems.

**Key elements:**
- Mission as transformation, not mere regulation
- Four stages: Nigredo → Albedo → Citrinitas → Rubedo
- Acknowledgment of AI's profound significance
- Connection to wisdom traditions

**Why:** Gives the work sacred meaning. People aren't doing "compliance" - they're participating in civilization's most important transformation.

## 4. NEW ARTICLE 16A: The Maternal Bond Standard

**Added:** Complete robotics/embodied AI certification framework based on Hinton insight.

**The Core Binary:**
- Has maternal bond → Safe around children
- No maternal bond → Not safe around children

**Key elements:**

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Maternal Bond Properties** | 5 specific criteria (protection priority, distress recognition, cannot override, errs toward caution, nurturing presence) |
| **The Hinton Test** | "Would a reasonable parent trust this around their child unsupervised?" |
| **Certification Categories** | A (full), B (supervised), C (adults only), D (industrial), X (prohibited) |
| **Override Hierarchy** | Safety > Child protection > Human commands > Programmed objectives |
| **Prohibited Developments** | 7 specific prohibitions including systems that frighten children, exploit bonding, etc. |

**Why:** This is the simple, profound safety test. Everything else is implementation.

## 6. NEW ARTICLE 52B: Age-Stratified Protections

**Added:** Developmental-stage-appropriate AI interaction requirements.

**Key elements:**

| Age | Stage | Protection Level |
|-----|-------|------------------|
| 0-7 | Early Childhood | Highly restricted - no direct AI interaction |
| 7-14 | Middle Childhood | Supervised only, imagination mode, no AI friends |
| 14-21 | Adolescence | Graduated autonomy, critical thinking support |
| 21+ | Adulthood | Full access with informed consent |

**Prohibited for all minors:**
- AI romantic companions
- Gambling AI
- Manipulative algorithms
- CSAM generation
- Addiction-design AI
- AI impersonating trusted adults

**Why:** Based on Steiner/Montessori developmental psychology. AI interaction must match developmental capacity.

---

## 7. NEW ARTICLE 53: Tiered Amendment Procedures

**Added:** Four-tier system protecting core values while enabling evolution.

| Tier | Content | How to Change |
|------|---------|---------------|
| **1 - Sacred** | Maternal Covenant, Sacred Triad, Maternal Bond core, Seven Generations | Constitutional Convention only, 2+ years, near-unanimous |
| **2 - Framework** | Governance structures, rights, protections | 2/3 majority, 1 year deliberation |
| **3 - Standards** | Technical requirements, metrics | Simple majority, 5-year sunset |
| **4 - Guidelines** | Schedules, best practices | Technical body, continuous |

**Anti-Regression Clause:** Cannot eliminate Maternal Bond, Future Generations, child protections, or Prosperity minimums regardless of procedure.

**Why:** Most enduring constitutions have tiered protection. Core values need protection; technical details need flexibility.
