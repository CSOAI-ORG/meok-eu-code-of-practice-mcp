## 1. The Power of LOIs: Turning Interest into Valuation

An LOI (Letter of Intent) is a **non-binding** document that signals serious interest from a potential customer. It is the single most powerful tool for a pre-revenue company to prove market demand.

### Why LOIs Work:

*   **De-risks Investment:** For an investor, the biggest risk is "Will anyone buy this?" An LOI from a reputable company is a resounding "YES!"
*   **Creates Forward Revenue:** While non-binding, LOIs represent a pipeline of future income. This "forward revenue" can be used in valuation models.
*   **Validates Product-Market Fit:** It proves you've built something people actually want, moving you from the "idea" stage to the "traction" stage.

### How We Calculate the Valuation Boost:

The standard formula for SaaS valuation is a multiple of Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR). For a pre-revenue AI company with strong IP, this is typically:

> **Valuation = (Monthly Recurring Revenue × 12) × 5-10x Multiplier**

An LOI for £1,000/month represents £12,000 in ARR. At a conservative 5x multiplier, that single LOI adds **£60,000** to your pre-money valuation.
