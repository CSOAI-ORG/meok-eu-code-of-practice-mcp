# TC260 AI Safety Governance Framework Research

## Source
- OneTrust: https://www.onetrust.com/blog/chinas-tc260-releases-ai-safety-governance-framework/

## Key Framework Components

### 1. Risk Management and Transparency
- Thorough impact assessments required before, during, and after deployment
- Identify potential biases
- Ensure data integrity
- Establish security mechanisms
- Continuous monitoring of AI outcomes
- Document AI models, data sources, and decision-making processes for auditing

### 2. Ethical Responsibilities
- Prioritize public welfare and minimize harm
- Account for societal and individual impacts
- Special attention to vulnerable groups
- Prevent discrimination
- Protect privacy
- Foster inclusivity

### 3. Accountability Mechanisms
- Clear accountability structures for unintended consequences
- Protocols for incident reporting and rectification
- Channels for external oversight
- Explainable AI systems (especially in high-stakes scenarios)

### 4. Cross-border Data, Security, and Privacy
- Safeguarding data across borders
- Compliance with China's Data Security Law and Personal Information Protection Law
- Cross-border data flow protocols
- Security rules from data collection to deletion
- User rights: control, information, choice

## Framework Objectives
- Promote responsible AI innovation
- Manage societal impact of AI systems
- Establish comprehensive governance structure
