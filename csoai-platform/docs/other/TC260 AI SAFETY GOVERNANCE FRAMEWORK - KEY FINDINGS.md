# TC260 AI SAFETY GOVERNANCE FRAMEWORK - KEY FINDINGS

**Source:** China's National Information Security Standardization Technical Committee (TC260)  
**Released:** September 9, 2024  
**URL:** https://www.onetrust.com/blog/chinas-tc260-releases-ai-safety-governance-framework/

---

## CORE PRINCIPLES

### 1. RISK MANAGEMENT & TRANSPARENCY
- Thorough impact assessments before, during, and after deployment
- Identify potential biases
- Ensure data integrity
- Establish security mechanisms
- Continuously monitor AI outcomes
- Document AI models, data sources, and decision-making processes
- Enable auditing

### 2. ETHICAL RESPONSIBILITIES
- Prioritize public welfare and minimize harm
- Account for societal and individual impacts
- Pay specific attention to vulnerable groups
- Prevent discrimination
- Protect privacy
- Foster inclusivity

### 3. ACCOUNTABILITY MECHANISMS
- Create clear accountability structures
- Address unintended consequences
- Establish incident reporting and rectification protocols
- Create channels for external oversight
- Ensure AI systems are explainable
- Make decision-making processes understandable to stakeholders

### 4. CROSS-BORDER DATA, SECURITY & PRIVACY
- Safeguard data across borders
- Comply with China's Data Security Law
- Comply with China's Personal Information Protection Law
- Prioritize cross-border data flow protocols
- Respect security rules from data collection to deletion
- Ensure user rights: control, informed consent, choice

---

## IMPLEMENTATION REQUIREMENTS

### GOVERNANCE TEAMS
- Establish dedicated AI governance teams
- Cross-functional involvement: legal, technical, privacy, operational experts
- Oversee compliance with TC260 framework

### TRAINING
- Invest in training programs
- Inform employees about AI safety, ethics, and governance
- Foster culture of responsibility

### AUDITS
- Perform detailed audits of current AI systems
- Ensure transparency, ethical, and accountability standards
- Update AI models to address shortcomings or risks

---

## STRATEGIC APPROACH

**Balance:** AI innovation + safety  
**Focus:** Transparency, accountability, security  
**Goal:** Anticipate and mitigate potential harms  
**Scope:** National security, public welfare, data privacy

---

## COMPARISON TO WESTERN REGULATIONS

**Similarities:**
- Focus on transparency and accountability
- Risk management requirements
- Ethical considerations
- Data privacy protection

**Unique to TC260:**
- Strong emphasis on national security
- Cross-border data flow restrictions
- Alignment with Chinese legal frameworks (Data Security Law, PIPL)
- Proactive governance structures

**Western Equivalents to Research:**
- EU AI Act
- US AI Executive Order
- UK AI White Paper
- Canada's AIDA
- Australia's AI Ethics Framework
