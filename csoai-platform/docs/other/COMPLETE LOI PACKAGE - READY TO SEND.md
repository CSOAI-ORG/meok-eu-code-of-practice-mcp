## 💡 PRO TIPS

### 1. Leverage Your Relationship
- Reference specific projects you worked on together
- Mention mutual contacts or shared experiences
- Keep the tone warm and personal, not salesy

### 2. Lead with Value
- Focus on the DWT 2026/2025 compliance angle (pain point)
- Emphasize time savings and efficiency gains
- Position as "exclusive early access" not "please sign this"

### 3. Make It Easy
- LOI is non-binding (emphasize this)
- No legal obligation or payment required
- Simple email confirmation is sufficient (no need for DocuSign unless they prefer)

### 4. Follow Up Strategically
- Wait 3-4 days before first follow-up
- Reference something specific from your original email
- Offer to answer any questions or concerns

### 5. Convert LOIs to Revenue
- Once signed, prioritize onboarding these clients
- Offer white-glove service and dedicated support
- Turn them into case studies and testimonials
