## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**SOAI (SafetyOf.AI)** is the consumer-facing (B2C) application of the COAI ecosystem. It is designed to be the **"McAfee for AI Safety"** - a simple, accessible, and powerful tool that protects everyday users from the risks of AI.

While COAI is the enterprise-grade governance framework for businesses and governments, SOAI is the **shield for the individual**. It leverages the power of the 33-agent COAI council to provide real-time protection and insights to the masses.

## 3. CORE FEATURES (POWERED BY COAI)

SOAI will be a simple, intuitive application (web, mobile, browser extension) with four core features:

### 3.1 **The Shield: Real-Time AI Threat Protection**

**How it works:**
- A browser extension that actively scans web pages, apps, and content for AI-related risks.
- When a potential risk is detected, SOAI sends it to the COAI council for a real-time vote.
- The user receives an instant notification: **"SAFE"** or **"WARNING"**.

**What it protects against:**
- **Deepfakes:** (Powered by **ProofOf.ai**) - "This video has a 92% probability of being a deepfake."
- **Bias:** (Powered by **BiasDetectionOf.ai**) - "This news article shows significant political bias."
- **Manipulation:** (Powered by COAI) - "This shopping site is using manipulative AI to rush your purchase."
- **Privacy Risks:** (Powered by **DataPrivacyOf.ai**) - "This app is collecting more data than it needs."
- **Scams:** (Powered by COAI) - "This email is likely an AI-generated phishing scam."

### 3.2 **The Scanner: On-Demand Content Analysis**

**How it works:**
- Users can upload any file (image, video, audio, text) or paste a URL into the SOAI app.
- SOAI sends the content to the COAI council for a comprehensive analysis.
- The user receives a detailed **"AI Safety Report"**.

**Example Use Cases:**
- **Parent:** Uploads a child's homework to check for AI-generated content.
- **Student:** Scans a research paper for bias and accuracy.
- **Journalist:** Verifies the authenticity of a video from a source.
- **Consumer:** Checks if a product review is a real person or an AI bot.

### 3.3 **The Vault: Personal Data Protection**

**How it works:**
- (Powered by **DataPrivacyOf.ai**)
- Scans the user's online accounts and data brokers to find where their personal data is being used by AI systems.
- Provides a simple one-click process to request data deletion under GDPR/CCPA.
- Monitors for data breaches involving AI systems.

### 3.4 **The Academy: AI Safety Education**

**How it works:**
- A curated feed of simple, easy-to-understand articles, videos, and tutorials on AI safety.
- **Content:**
  - "How to spot a deepfake in 30 seconds"
  - "Is your smart speaker listening to you?"
  - "The top 5 AI scams to watch out for"
- Powered by insights from the COAI council and real-world data.

- **SOAI Free:**
