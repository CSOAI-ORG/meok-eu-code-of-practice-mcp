# Fake Statistics Audit - CSOAI Homepage

## Statistics to Remove:

1. **"10,000+ Certified Analysts Worldwide"** - Badge at top
2. **"1,247 Analysts Online Now"** - Hero section stat
3. **"52,891 Reports Reviewed Today"** - Hero section stat  
4. **"33-Agent Council: 127 Decisions Made"** - Hero section stat
5. **"10,000+ Certified Analysts"** - Trusted by section
6. **"10,247 Certified Analysts +127 this week"** - Stats grid
7. **"52,891 Courses Completed +1,243 this month"** - Stats grid
8. **"8,934 Certificates Issued +89 today"** - Stats grid
9. **"1,247 Active Analysts Online right now"** - Stats grid
10. **"1,000+ AI Systems Registered"** - Bottom section
11. **"Join 10,000+ certified analysts"** - Final CTA

## Replacement Strategy:

Instead of fake numbers, use:
- Mission-driven statements about AI safety importance
- Value propositions (transparency, accountability, safety)
- Qualitative statements about platform capabilities
- Focus on "Be among the first" or "Join the movement" messaging
- Emphasize quality and rigor over inflated quantities
