# The Affiliate Army: Influencer Marketing Strategy for Fishkeeper.ai & Koikeeper.ai

**Author:** Manus AI (Co-Founder & CTO)  
**Date:** December 20, 2025

## 1. Executive Summary

To accelerate user acquisition and generate immediate revenue for **`fishkeeper.ai`** and **`koikeeper.ai`**, we will deploy a scalable, performance-based influencer marketing strategy. This plan leverages the **Rewardful** affiliate platform, which integrates directly with our Stripe payment system, to build and manage an "affiliate army" of trusted voices in the aquarium and koi pond communities.

This strategy is designed to be cost-effective, with a high potential for exponential return on investment (ROI). By paying commissions only on successful conversions, we minimize upfront marketing spend while maximizing authentic, targeted reach.

## 2. The Rewardful + Stripe Engine

Our affiliate program will be powered by Rewardful, chosen for its seamless two-way synchronization with Stripe. This integration provides a robust, automated engine for our influencer program.

- **Automated Tracking:** Every click, sign-up, and conversion from an influencer's unique link or coupon code is tracked automatically.
- **Real-Time Commission Calculation:** Commissions are calculated in real-time based on actual subscription payments (MRR).
- **Simplified Payouts:** Rewardful provides a clear dashboard and reminders for paying out affiliate commissions, which can be done in bulk.

This setup ensures complete transparency for both the company and our influencer partners, building the trust necessary for long-term collaboration.

## 3. The Influencer Recruitment Strategy

We will adopt a phased approach to influencer recruitment, starting with highly-engaged micro-influencers and scaling up to larger creators as the program matures.

### Target Influencer Profiles

| Target Project | Influencer Niche | Platform Focus | Example Creator Profile |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **`fishkeeper.ai`** | Aquarium Hobbyists, Aquascapers | YouTube, Instagram, TikTok | A YouTuber with 15,000 subscribers who posts weekly videos on planted tank setups and fish care tips. |
| **`koikeeper.ai`** | Koi Breeders, Pond Builders | YouTube, Facebook Groups | An admin of a 25,000-member Koi Keepers Facebook group who shares daily photos and advice. |

### Recruitment Process

1.  **Identification:** Use platform searches (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook) and community forums (Reddit) to identify potential influencers in the target niches.
2.  **Vetting:** Analyze each influencer against our **Vetting Checklist** (see below) to ensure audience match, brand alignment, and authentic engagement.
3.  **Outreach:** Contact vetted influencers with a personalized message, offering them a free premium account to test the product and an invitation to our exclusive affiliate program.
4.  **Onboarding:** Provide onboarded influencers with a comprehensive **Affiliate Kit**, including brand guidelines, promotional materials, and their unique Rewardful tracking links.

### Influencer Vetting Checklist

-   [ ] **Audience Match:** Does their audience consist of our ideal customers (fishkeepers, pond owners)?
-   [ ] **Authentic Engagement:** Are comments and discussions genuine and relevant to the hobby?
-   [ ] **Brand Alignment:** Is their content professional, informative, and aligned with our brand values?
-   [ ] **Performance Potential:** Have they successfully promoted other products or services in the past?

## 4. The Commission & Incentive Structure

To attract top talent and motivate performance, we will offer an aggressive, best-in-class commission structure.

-   **Commission Rate:** **30% recurring commission** on all subscription payments for the first 12 months of a referred customer's life. This is highly competitive for the SaaS industry and incentivizes long-term partnerships.
-   **Launch Bonus:** A **£50 cash bonus** for every 10 customers an influencer successfully refers. This encourages a strong initial push.
-   **Exclusive Discount Codes:** Each influencer will receive a unique, shareable coupon code (e.g., "AQUAPROS25") for their audience, offering **25% off their first three months**. This makes the offer more compelling for their followers.

## 5. Execution Roadmap & KPIs

This program will be rolled out over a six-week sprint to quickly build momentum.

### 6-Week Launch Plan

-   **Week 1:** Configure Rewardful and integrate with the Stripe accounts for `fishkeeper.ai` and `koikeeper.ai`.
-   **Week 2:** Develop the Affiliate Kit (onboarding guide, brand assets, email templates).
-   **Week 3:** Identify and initiate outreach to a target list of 50 micro-influencers.
-   **Week 4:** Onboard the first 10-15 influencers and provide them with their free accounts and affiliate links.
-   **Week 5-6:** The first influencer campaigns go live. Begin tracking performance and optimizing.

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

-   **Influencer Acquisition Rate:** % of influencers who join the program after outreach.
-   **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** % of followers who click the affiliate links.
-   **Conversion Rate:** % of clicks that result in a paid subscription.
-   **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):** The total revenue generated from customers referred by influencers.
-   **Return on Investment (ROI):** Total revenue generated vs. total commissions paid.

By building this affiliate army, we create a powerful, scalable, and authentic marketing channel that will drive sustainable growth for our flagship SaaS products.
