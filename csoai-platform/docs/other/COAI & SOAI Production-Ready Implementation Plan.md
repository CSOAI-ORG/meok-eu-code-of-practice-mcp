## 2. THE 12-MONTH ROADMAP

This roadmap is divided into four quarters, each with a clear strategic focus.

### **Q1 2026: Build the Core & Launch the Shield (Jan-Mar)**

**Focus:** Build the core COAI framework and launch the first SOAI consumer product.

| Month | Key Deliverables |
|---|---|
| **January** | **COAI Core Framework MVP:** 33-agent council, voting mechanism, PDCA loop, governance dashboard. **SOAI Browser Extension MVP:** "The Shield" with deepfake detection (powered by ProofOf.ai). |

| **March** | **COAI NIST AI RMF Module:** Automated compliance with US framework. **SOAI v1.0 Launch:** Official launch of browser extension with freemium model. |

**Q1 Goal:** COAI core functional, SOAI v1.0 launched, 10,000+ users, first revenue.

### **Q2 2026: Expand the Ecosystem & Secure First Enterprise Pilot (Apr-Jun)**

**Focus:** Integrate specialized modules and land the first enterprise pilot customer for COAI.

| Month | Key Deliverables |
|---|---|
| **April** | **Integrate 3 Modules:** BiasDetectionOf.ai, TransparencyOf.ai, DataPrivacyOf.ai. **SOAI Mobile App (iOS/Android):** Launch with all 4 core features. |
| **May** | **First Enterprise Pilot:** Onboard one Fortune 500 company to use COAI for EU AI Act compliance. **SOAI Family Plan:** Launch multi-device subscription. |
| **June** | **Integrate 3 More Modules:** AccountabilityOf.ai, AGISafe.ai, AISecurity.AI. **SOAI Partnerships:** Announce first antivirus/VPN bundle partnership. |

**Q2 Goal:** First enterprise revenue, 100,000+ SOAI users, full ecosystem integration.

### **Q3 2026: Scale Enterprise & Prepare for EU AI Act Enforcement (Jul-Sep)**

**Focus:** Scale enterprise sales and prepare for the EU AI Act enforcement deadline (August 2026).

| Month | Key Deliverables |
|---|---|
| **July** | **COAI Enterprise Launch:** Official launch of the full COAI platform. **EU AI Act Marketing Campaign:** Target all high-risk AI providers in the EU. |
| **August** | **EU AI Act Enforcement:** COAI is the #1 solution for compliance. **Onboard 10 Enterprise Customers:** Target €50K MRR. |
| **September** | **Integrate Final Modules:** SuicideStop.AI, EthicalGovernanceOf.AI. **SOAI 2.0:** Major update with new features and improved UI. |

**Q3 Goal:** £50K MRR from COAI, 500,000+ SOAI users, market leadership in EU.

### **Q4 2026: Global Expansion & AGI Safety Leadership (Oct-Dec)**

**Focus:** Expand to US market and establish leadership in AGI safety.

| Month | Key Deliverables |
|---|---|
| **October** | **US Market Launch:** Target US enterprises with NIST AI RMF compliance. **Publish AGI Safety Whitepaper:** Based on AGISafe.ai research. |
| **November** | **Onboard 20 US Enterprise Customers:** Target £150K MRR. **Partner with AGI Research Labs:** Provide COAI for AGI safety research. |

**Q4 Goal:** £150K MRR, 1M+ users, global standard for AI safety.
