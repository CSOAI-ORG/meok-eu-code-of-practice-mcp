## BACKGROUND

A. The Assignor is the sole creator, developer, and owner of certain intellectual property rights relating to software platforms, domain names, artificial intelligence systems, and associated business assets (collectively, the "**Intellectual Property**").

B. The Assignor wishes to assign all right, title, and interest in and to the Intellectual Property to the Assignee.

C. The Assignee wishes to acquire all such rights from the Assignor.

---

## IT IS AGREED AS FOLLOWS

### 1. DEFINITIONS AND INTERPRETATION

**1.1** In this Agreement, unless the context otherwise requires:

**"Intellectual Property"** means all intellectual property rights of any kind throughout the world, whether registered or unregistered, including but not limited to:

*   **(a) The Software:** All source code, object code, algorithms, databases, user interfaces, APIs, documentation, and related materials for the 23 software platforms listed in **Schedule 1**, including all versions, updates, and derivatives thereof.

*   **(b) The Domains:** The 23 internet domain names listed in **Schedule 2**, including all associated DNS records, hosting accounts, and related digital assets.

*   **(c) The RLMAI Backend:** The proprietary reinforcement learning multi-agent intelligence (RLMAI) system, including all source code, trained models, algorithms, architectures, training data, documentation, trade secrets, know-how, and confidential information.

*   **(d) The Business Models:** All business plans, financial models, market research, customer data, strategic documentation, marketing materials, brand assets, and operational procedures related to the platforms listed in Schedule 1.

*   **(e) All Associated Rights:** All copyrights, database rights, design rights, patents, trade marks, trade secrets, know-how, and all other intellectual property rights of whatever nature relating to or arising from the above.

### 2. ASSIGNMENT

**2.1** In consideration of the sum of **£10.00 (ten pounds sterling)**, the receipt and sufficiency of which the Assignor hereby acknowledges, the Assignor hereby irrevocably and unconditionally assigns to the Assignee with full title guarantee:

*   (a) The entire right, title, and interest in and to the Intellectual Property throughout the world;
*   (b) All rights to sue for past, present, and future infringements of the Intellectual Property; and
*   (c) All revenues, royalties, damages, and other benefits arising from or in connection with the Intellectual Property.

**2.2** The assignment under clause 2.1 shall take effect immediately upon execution of this Agreement and shall be for the full period of copyright and all other intellectual property rights, including any extensions and renewals thereof.

### 3. WARRANTIES

**3.1** The Assignor warrants and represents to the Assignee that:

*   (a) The Assignor is the sole legal and beneficial owner of the Intellectual Property;
*   (b) The Assignor has full power and authority to enter into this Agreement and to assign the Intellectual Property;
*   (c) The Intellectual Property is free from all encumbrances, charges, liens, and third-party rights;
*   (d) The Assignor has not granted any licences or rights to any third party in respect of the Intellectual Property;
*   (e) To the best of the Assignor's knowledge, the Intellectual Property does not infringe the intellectual property rights of any third party.

### 4. FURTHER ASSURANCE

**4.1** The Assignor shall, at the request and expense of the Assignee, promptly execute all documents and do all acts and things as may be reasonably required by the Assignee to:

*   (a) Perfect the assignment made by this Agreement;
*   (b) Vest the Intellectual Property in the Assignee;
*   (c) Enable the Assignee to register its title to the Intellectual Property; and
*   (d) Defend the Intellectual Property against third-party claims.

### 5. CONFIDENTIALITY

**5.1** The Assignor shall keep confidential all information relating to the Intellectual Property and shall not disclose such information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Assignee.

### 6. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

**6.1** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of England and Wales.

**6.2** The parties irrevocably agree that the courts of England and Wales shall have exclusive jurisdiction to settle any dispute or claim arising out of or in connection with this Agreement.
