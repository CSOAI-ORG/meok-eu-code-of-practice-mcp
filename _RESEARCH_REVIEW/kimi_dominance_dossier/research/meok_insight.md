**Derived From**:
- Dim01: MCP is tool access, not business process orchestration
- Dim02: A2A is agent-to-agent, not library-to-process
- Dim09: Every vertical needs library integration into business workflows
- Dim12: lib2bp has NO direct competitor — greenfield

**Rationale**: Enterprises have millions of libraries (npm, PyPI, Maven) but no standard way to turn them into business processes. **Implications**: Define and publish the lib2bp specification. Position it as complementary to MCP and A2A. Build the reference implementation. Donate to Linux Foundation for legitimacy. Capture value through managed process orchestration.
