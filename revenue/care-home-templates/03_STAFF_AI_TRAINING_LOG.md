## Required modules

Every staff member must complete the following before first use of any AI tool in their role:

| # | Module | Duration | Required for |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **AI Literacy Module 1 — what AI is, how it can fail, what to do when it does** | 30 min | All staff using any AI tool |
| 2 | **GDPR Refresher — special-category data, consent, breach reporting** | 30 min | All staff |
| 3 | **Tool-specific module** (one per high-risk tool the staff member operates) | 15-30 min each | Staff operating high-risk tools (medication, fall detection, etc.) |
| 4 | **Annual refresher** | 30 min | All staff, every 12 months |

Materials sourced from: MEOK AI Labs `meok-eu-ai-act-mcp` Article 4 literacy template; supplemented with home-specific content as required.

## Materials provided with this pack

The following AI Literacy training materials are provided ready-to-use:

1. **AI Literacy Module 1 — slide deck (10 slides, 30-min facilitated session)**
   - What AI actually does (vs marketing claims)
   - How AI fails (drift, hallucination, training-data bias)
   - What to do when an AI gives a strange answer
   - When to escalate to a human + manager
   - Staff quiz (5 questions, pass at 4/5)

2. **GDPR Refresher — slide deck (8 slides, 30-min facilitated session)**
   - Special-category data definition
   - Consent vs other lawful bases
   - Breach reporting — 24h internal, 72h ICO
   - Resident rights at a glance
