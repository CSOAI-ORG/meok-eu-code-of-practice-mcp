### 1. Standard identity + contact data
- Name, date of birth, address, NHS number

### 2. Special-category health data (GDPR Article 9)
- Medical conditions, medications, allergies
- GP, hospital, district nurse details
- Care needs assessment results
- Mental capacity assessments

- Mobility and falls history

### 3. Operational data
- Care plan notes + activity records
- Incident records (falls, behaviour, near-misses)
- Family visit log

- AI tool outputs that reference you (see §6 below)

## Lawful basis for processing

We rely on the following lawful bases (UK GDPR Article 6 + Article 9):

- **Article 6(1)(b) — contract performance** for residency services
- **Article 6(1)(c) — legal obligation** for CQC reporting, safeguarding referrals, financial reporting
- **Article 6(1)(d) — vital interests** in medical emergencies
- **Article 6(1)(f) — legitimate interests** for care service improvement (limited and balanced against your rights)
- **Article 9(2)(h) — health-and-social-care provision** for health data
- **Article 9(2)(c) — vital interests** where you are unable to give consent
- **Article 9(2)(g) — substantial public interest** for safeguarding referrals

For photographs, marketing material, AI-tool data sharing, and any optional service, we rely on your **explicit, freely-given consent** (Article 9(2)(a)).

## How we use your information

We use your information to:

1. Provide your care safely and as agreed in your Care Plan
2. Communicate with you, your family, and your GP / NHS / specialists
3. Process payments and benefits
4. Comply with regulatory obligations (CQC, safeguarding, reporting)
5. Investigate and respond to incidents or complaints
6. Improve our service via internal audit + reflection
7. (With explicit consent) take and use photographs for the home or family communication

## Who we share your information with

We share your information **only** with:

- **Health and social care professionals** involved in your care (GP, district nurse, hospital, specialist, social worker, hospice, etc.)
- **Family / next-of-kin** as you have nominated
- **Local authority and CCG / ICB** as required for funding and oversight
- **CQC** as required for regulatory inspection
- **Safeguarding teams** where there is a concern under the Care Act 2014
- **Coroner + police** where legally required
- **Insurers + auditors** under confidentiality
- **AI tool suppliers** that we have registered (see §6 below) — only the data essential for the tool to do its registered purpose

We **do not sell** your personal information. We **do not use it for marketing** without your specific opt-in consent.

## Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

- **Be informed** about how we use your data (this notice)
- **Access** your data — Subject Access Request (SAR), free, response within 1 month
- **Rectification** of inaccurate data
- **Erasure** ("right to be forgotten") — subject to clinical/legal retention
- **Restriction** of processing in certain circumstances
- **Data portability** for data you have provided
- **Object** to certain processing (in particular legitimate-interest-based processing)
- **Not be subject** to solely-automated decisions about you (Article 22)
- **Withdraw consent** at any time for consent-based processing
