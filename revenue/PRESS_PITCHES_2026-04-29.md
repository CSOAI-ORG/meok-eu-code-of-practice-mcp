## Sending discipline

- **Send all 5 in same week, NOT same day** — Tuesday/Wednesday/Thursday spread, max 2 per day
- **Subject lines tested for length + hook** — under 50 chars, no emojis (UK trade press scams emojis as marketing)
- **Reply to ANY response within 24 hours** with the assets requested — editors disappear when slow
- **Track responses in a spreadsheet** — date sent, date opened, date replied, outcome
- **If no reply in 7 business days, ONE follow-up** — short, single line: "Hi {name}, anything I can add to make this fit a piece? Happy to be brief."
