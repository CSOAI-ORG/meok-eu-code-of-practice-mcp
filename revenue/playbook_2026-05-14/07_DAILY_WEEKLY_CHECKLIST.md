# 07 — Daily & Weekly Checklist

**Pin this to the fridge.** Simple recurring rhythms that build the business without burning anyone out.

---

## Wendy + Fred — Daily (5 minutes per day)

- [ ] Ask one patient for a Google review (with a QR-code card)
- [ ] Hand one patient a microfibre cleaning cloth with your phone number
- [ ] Note any patient comments in the "story file" (a simple notebook for press/marketing material later)

That's it. Five minutes total.

---

## Wendy + Fred — Weekly (1 hour total)

- [ ] **Monday morning (15 min):** Check email for grant updates, NHS pathway news, press enquiries
- [ ] **Tuesday/Wednesday (20 min):** Visit one care home OR one local GP surgery for relationship building
- [ ] **Thursday (15 min):** Make one post on practice Facebook page (patient story, eye health fact, behind-the-scenes)
- [ ] **Friday (10 min):** Review week's bookings + reviews + Care Plan signups. Plan one thing for next week.

---

## Wendy + Fred — Monthly (3 hours total)

- [ ] **First week of month:** 1-hour family meeting with Nick to review business progress + grants pipeline
- [ ] **Second week:** 1-hour open conversation with one new contact (potential apprentice, partner, GP, care-home manager)
- [ ] **Third week:** 1-hour reading time — read one document from this playbook OR the master grants doc
- [ ] **Fourth week:** 1-hour reflection — write down 3 things that worked + 1 thing to change

---

## Wendy + Fred — Quarterly (half day)

- [ ] **Quarter end:** Half-day review — what's working, what isn't, adjust plan
- [ ] Submit press story to local newspaper (whatever the practice has done in the quarter)
- [ ] Send a thank-you note to top 5 referrers (GP, care home manager, loyal patient)
- [ ] Update the practice website with anything new (apprentice hire, new pathway, equipment, press coverage)

---

## Nick — Daily (1 hour)

- [ ] Check grants pipeline (15 min)
- [ ] Build/ship MEOK code or write CSO AI content (45 min) — actual product work, not admin

---

## Nick — Weekly (4 hours total)

- [ ] **Monday:** Plan week + check deadlines (30 min)
- [ ] **Tuesday-Thursday:** Deep work on highest-priority grant or product (2 hours)
- [ ] **Friday:** Family update + review with Wendy + Fred + admin (1 hour)
- [ ] **Saturday:** Visit the farm + practical work (3 hrs — physical work not admin)
- [ ] **Sunday:** Read one playbook / case study / research paper (1 hour)

---

## Family — Monthly

- [ ] **First Sunday of month:** Family lunch with all three present + dogs. No phones at the table. Talk through one specific business question, no more.
- [ ] **Mid-month:** Email summary from Nick to Wendy + Fred — 1 page max, what shipped, what's next, what they should know
- [ ] **End of month:** Family financial review — joint look at:
  - Templeman Opticians monthly P&L
  - MEOK MRR
  - Grants pipeline status
  - Family trust + FIC contributions
  - Personal benefits + entitlements being claimed

---

## What you must NEVER skip

Three things, every week, no exceptions:

1. **The Friday family review** — keeps everyone in sync. Without this, drift sets in.
2. **The Tuesday GP/care-home visit** — referrals are the lifeblood of the practice.
3. **The daily Google review ask** — reviews compound exponentially. Skip a week = lose 5-10 lifetime reviews.

---

## What you can skip if life gets in the way

- A weekly Facebook post can become monthly
- Quarterly press story can become twice-yearly
- The monthly family lunch can move to phone if necessary

**But never skip the three core rhythms above.**

---

## Emergency rules

If anyone is overwhelmed:

1. **Stop everything except patient care + family.** Take a week off all marketing/admin/grants.
2. **Call Farming Community Network 03000 111 999** (7am-11pm) — free, confidential, no judgement.
3. **Reset by reading 00_START_HERE_README.md** again from the top.

The plan is 5 years, not 5 days. Pacing matters more than speed.
