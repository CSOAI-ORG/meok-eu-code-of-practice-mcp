## What Wendy + Fred can ask Nick when they don't understand

"Nick, what's an MCP?"
→ A small piece of software that AI assistants use to do specialist tasks. Like a tool in a toolbox. MEOK builds tools for AI compliance.

"What's a grant?"
→ Money from government or charity that you don't have to pay back, given to encourage useful work. Not a loan.

"What's the MCP Registry?"
→ The official Apple App Store equivalent for these AI tools. Get listed there and developers find you.

"What's R&D tax credit?"
→ HMRC gives you back about 15-27% of money you spent on research and development. Cash refund.

"What's a Family Investment Company?"
→ A regular Ltd company that holds family wealth. Trust-like benefits, simpler tax. Wendy + Fred own voting shares, Nick gets growth shares = future value goes to Nick outside their estate.

"What's an IIP Trust?"
→ Interest in Possession trust. Wendy + Fred have lifetime right to live in the farmhouse + receive farm income. Nick is the remainder beneficiary. Protects assets from MEOK/Optician liability + saves IHT.

"What's an LDC?"
→ Lawful Development Certificate. A piece of paper from the council saying "this thing on your land is legal." Costs £155-309. Settles arguments forever.

"What's BNG?"
→ Biodiversity Net Gain. Housing developers must offset 10% extra biodiversity. They can BUY biodiversity from landowners like us. £80-150k lump sum for the 6.5 acres in theory.

"Why is this all open source?"
→ Because it's the fastest way to build distribution + trust. Red Hat sold $34bn of support on top of free Linux. MEOK sells support + certification + indemnity on top of free MCPs.
