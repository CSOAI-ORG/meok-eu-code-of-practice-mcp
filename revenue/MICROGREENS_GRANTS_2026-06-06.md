## The positioning (reusable across applications)
> A controlled-environment **microgreens** operation on a Norfolk farm (IOK, ~2.63 ha): high-value, year-round local food production; on-farm **diversification** of an existing holding; **agri-tech + automation** (IoT environment monitoring, 3D-printed tooling); **compliance-led** (HACCP + traceable, signed batch evidence) supplying local restaurants/retail. Outcomes: local food security, skilled rural jobs, reduced food miles, climate-resilient indoor growing.

This positioning hits the three things UK ag/rural grants reward: **diversification**, **innovation/productivity**, and **local economic + environmental benefit**.

## Drafting checklist (when a real window confirms)
1. **Eligibility gate:** confirm the holding/County Parish Holding number, business status, the call's exact eligible-cost list.
2. **Costed BOM:** racking, LED, irrigation, sensors, cold storage, fit-out — get 2–3 quotes (most grants need quotes).
3. **Outcomes with numbers:** projected yield, jobs created (FTE), local buyers (LOIs help), food-miles reduced, £ turnover.
4. **The innovation hook:** IoT environment logging + **signed/traceable batch HACCP evidence** (the CSOAI attestation tie-in) — this is what lifts it above "another polytunnel."
5. **Match funding:** most capital grants need 40–60% match — confirm what you can put in.
