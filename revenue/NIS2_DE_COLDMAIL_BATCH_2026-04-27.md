## Targeting (per audit)

NIS2-UmsuCG covers German entities in:
- Energy, transport, water, healthcare, ICT, food production (Section 30 essential)
- Manufacturing, postal/courier, waste, chemicals, research (Section 32 important)
- DSP, MSPs, B2B SaaS with German customers
- 50+ employees OR >€10M turnover

Of estimated 30K obligated entities, only ~11.5K registered by 6 March 2026 deadline.
**~17,500 are non-compliant right now.**

## Send rules

- Use a fresh sender domain (csoai.org) with SPF/DKIM/DMARC verified.
- Max 50 sends/day from one mailbox to avoid spam triggers.
- Personalize {Company} + {FirstName} for each.
- Track opens via PostHog or Mailtrack (free).
- DO NOT add unsubscribe link — these are 1:1 B2B emails, not bulk marketing.
- DO NOT CC anyone. DO NOT BCC.
