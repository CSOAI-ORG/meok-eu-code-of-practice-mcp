## Anti-patterns to avoid

❌ **Don't follow up the same day** — looks desperate
❌ **Don't BCC multiple prospects** — instant spam flag
❌ **Don't link to /pricing** as primary CTA — too high-friction, link to specific MCP landing
❌ **Don't claim "guaranteed compliance"** — legal risk
❌ **Don't send before 08:00 or after 18:00 BST** — bin filters
❌ **Don't include images or attachments** — drops deliverability
❌ **Don't use "follow up", "checking in", "circling back"** — instant delete signal

✅ **Do** send within 48 hours of researching the prospect (recency boost)
✅ **Do** include a specific compliance deadline in the subject
✅ **Do** provide both a free option AND a paid option
✅ **Do** include verifiable identity (CSOAI LTD + CRN)
✅ **Do** track via UTM-tagged URLs (which I've embedded above)
