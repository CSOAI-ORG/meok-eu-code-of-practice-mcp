## The brutal truth about domain portfolios

25 .ai domains feels like wealth. It isn't. **A parked domain has the same enterprise value as a piece of paper.** Each domain is either:
1. **Producing revenue** (paid product, lead magnet → conversion, affiliate income)
2. **Producing SEO equity** (backlinks, organic traffic, ranks for keywords that monetise elsewhere)
3. **Holding optionality** (about to be sold, donated for PR, or activated)
4. **Bleeding £25/yr** (renewal cost with nothing returning)

The job is to move all 25 out of category 4 and into 1-3 within 90 days.
