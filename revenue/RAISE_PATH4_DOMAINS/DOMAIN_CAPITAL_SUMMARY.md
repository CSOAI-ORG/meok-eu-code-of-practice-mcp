## 1. Why the numbers are small (no fantasy pricing)

- Median .ai aftermarket sale in 2025 was **~$811 (£640)**; the eye-catching averages ($6,525) are pulled up by one-word generics (`cloud.ai` = $600K) that bear no resemblance to a compound name like `ethicalgovernanceof.ai`.
- Nick's saleable names are **parked, low-traffic, two-word/compound brandables** — the segment that appraises at **£100–£500** and transacts near the **low end of any range**, often only after months of listing.
- So the expected outcome is **most names sell at their min-offer, a couple don't sell at all in 12 months, and maybe one reaches £1K+**. The "high" column assumes the right operator finds the best name (loopfactory / pokerhud / commercialvehicle) — possible, not bankable.
