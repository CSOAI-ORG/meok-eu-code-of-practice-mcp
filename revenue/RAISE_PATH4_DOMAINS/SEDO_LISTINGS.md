## Listing mechanics (quick reference)

- **Dan.com (now part of GoDaddy):** best for clean two-word brandables; buyer-friendly checkout, lease-to-own option, 9–15% commission. Use for #1, #2, #3, and the swap-in.
- **Sedo:** largest reach, good for governance/keyword names and .vc; ~15–20% commission, slower. Use for #4, #5, #6, #7, and as a mirror everywhere.
- **Afternic:** distributes to registrar search results (GoDaddy network) — good passive exposure; mirror #4–#6 here.
- **NamePros "Domains for Sale":** free, niche-buyer reach — worth a thread for #2 (pokerhud) where the buyer community is specific.
- **Set every name to "make offer + BIN"**, not BIN-only — for compound brandables, ~all sales come from negotiated offers.
- **Don't pay for premium/featured placement.** At these price points the fees eat the proceeds.
