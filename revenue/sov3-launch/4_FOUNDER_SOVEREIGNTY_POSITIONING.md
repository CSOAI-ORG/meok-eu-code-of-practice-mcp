## A. The core claim

> **SOV3 is founder-owned. No investors, no exit clock, no board pressure to harvest you. The governance platform you trust shouldn't itself be someone's portfolio company waiting to be sold.**

Why it lands:
- **It's verifiable** (100% ownership — you can state it flatly).
- **It's structural** — a VC-backed rival literally cannot match "we have no investors" without giving back their funding. This is a moat of *kind*, not degree.
- **It reframes their strength as a liability** without a single false word: their $85M isn't fake, it's *a clock*. Investors need a 10x exit. That means price hikes, acquisition, or shutdown on the investors' timeline — not yours.
