## Sending discipline

- **Max 6 connection-request notes/day** (LinkedIn velocity limit ~10-15/day for cold; stay well under)
- **Wait 48h after connection** before first DM
- **One follow-up after 5 days** if no reply, then drop it
- **Personalize the connection note** with one specific reference to their content/work — never generic
- **Track in spreadsheet:** Name | Firm | Sent date | Connected date | Replied? | Outcome
