# EU AI Act Compliance Checklist 2026

**Deadline: August 2, 2026 | Days remaining: 104**

The EU AI Act (Regulation 2024/1689) is the world's first comprehensive AI law. Non-compliance penalties reach **€35 million or 7% of global turnover**.

## Step 1: Inventory Your AI Systems

- [ ] List every AI system in your organization
- [ ] Include third-party AI tools you use
- [ ] Note which ones process EU citizen data

## Step 2: Classify Risk Level

| Category | Examples | Requirements |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| **Prohibited** | Social scoring, subliminal manipulation | Must discontinue |
| **High Risk** | Hiring AI, credit scoring, medical diagnosis | Full compliance package |
| **Limited Risk** | Chatbots, content recommendation | Transparency obligations |
| **Minimal Risk** | Spam filters, games | No obligations |

## Step 3: Documentation (High Risk Only)

- [ ] Technical documentation (Article 11)
- [ ] Data governance procedures (Article 10)
- [ ] Record-keeping and logging (Article 12)
- [ ] Transparency information for users (Article 13)
- [ ] Human oversight mechanisms (Article 14)
- [ ] Accuracy and robustness testing (Article 15)
- [ ] Quality management system (Article 17)

## Step 4: Ongoing Monitoring

- [ ] Bias detection and monitoring
- [ ] Performance logging
- [ ] Incident reporting procedures
- [ ] Post-market monitoring plan
