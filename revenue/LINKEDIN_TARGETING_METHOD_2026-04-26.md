# How to find 10 verifiable LinkedIn targets — methodology, not fabrication

**Why this is a methodology, not a list:** I asked an agent to scrape 10 specific named EU compliance officers. The agent rightly declined — fabricated names + hallucinated post-personalisation hooks would be worse than no list, especially in the small EU compliance community that talks to itself. One bad DM = brand damage in a small market.

**Use these 5 sources, in priority order, to build your real list:**

## 1. LinkedIn Sales Navigator (paid, fastest, most reliable)
- 1-week free trial available
- Filter: Job title `("DPO" OR "Data Protection Officer" OR "Head of AI Governance" OR "Compliance Lead")`
- Geography: Germany OR Netherlands OR France OR Ireland
- Company size: 50-500 employees
- Activity filter: posted about "AI Act" / "NIS2" / "DORA" in last 30 days
- **Output:** ~50-200 names. You manually pick the 10 strongest.

## 2. IAPP member + speaker directories (free, verified)
- https://iapp.org/connect/find-a-member/ — searchable, members self-publish their roles
- https://iapp.org/conference/global-privacy-summit/ — speaker pages list real names + companies + LinkedIn
- AIGP certified directory: https://iapp.org/certify/aigp/
- **Output:** real names of professionals who *opted in* to being findable. Extremely high quality.

## 3. NIS2 Bundesanzeiger / BSI public consultations (free, German Mittelstand focus)
- BSI public consultation responses are signed by named compliance officers at named companies
- Search "BSI Konsultation NIS2" + the section number
- **Output:** verified Mittelstand DPOs/CISOs who proactively engaged with the regulator. Highest possible buyer intent.

## 4. Your own LinkedIn feed (warmest leads)
- Look at the last 30 days of posts you've liked/commented on, especially anything tagged #AIAct #NIS2 #DORA #GDPR
- Anyone who replied to your comment with substance is a warm lead
- **Output:** 5-15 high-EV warm contacts you already have implicit relationships with

## 5. Conference speaker pages (deterministic, free)
- Pick a specific event with an EU AI Act / DORA / NIS2 focus:
  - **IAPP Data Protection Intensive Brussels** (May 2026)
  - **CPDP Brussels** (Jan 2026 already passed; Jan 2027 CFP opens summer 2026)
  - **EU AI Act Compliance Summit** (search current event listings)
  - **DORA Live London / Frankfurt** (Q2 2026)
- Send me the URL of the speaker list and I'll WebFetch + extract real names + you do the LinkedIn lookup
- **Output:** verified speakers = high-credibility audience
