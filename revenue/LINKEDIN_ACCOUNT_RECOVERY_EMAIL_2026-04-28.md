## Honest realism first

LinkedIn's official policy:
- Deleted accounts can be restored within **14 days** of deletion (full data: connections, posts, messages, recommendations).
- Beyond 14 days, account is permanently purged and cannot be recovered.
- However, **if you can prove the deletion was made under coercion, fraud, or unauthorized access**, LinkedIn's Trust & Safety team has discretion to restore even older accounts.
