### Preliminary corrections — high-confidence facts before deep research lands

✅ **10-year rule (LURA 2023 s.115)** — existing polytunnels in place 10+ years uncontested have **immune** lawful use. Cannot be enforced against. Replacement on the same footprint is not new development.

✅ **TCPA 1990 s.55** — "development" is operations on land changing its character. Like-for-like replacement / re-skinning of existing polytunnel is **repair/maintenance**, not development. No planning needed.

✅ **GPDO 2015 Part 6 Class B** — agricultural building extension up to **10% cubic content** + max 465 m² ground area is permitted development with prior notification only (~£96 fee). Workshop extension fits here if workshop is agricultural in use.

✅ **TCPA s.55(2)(d) "incidental to dwellinghouse enjoyment"** — buildings within curtilage used incidentally to dwelling (greenhouses, hobby workshops, polytunnels for personal food) are NOT development. **Hancock v Secretary of State [2017]** confirmed substantial uses can qualify.

✅ **No commercial trade on site** changes:
- No business rates implications (it's domestic / agricultural)
- No VAT registration
- No Environment Agency permit for food trade
- No food safety registration
- No highways / transport assessment (no customers)
- Likely no need for commercial planning class at all

✅ **Suggested cheapest path** (subject to deep research confirmation):
1. **LDC s.191 (existing use)** for the polytunnel footprint — proves 10+ year established use (~£300-500)
2. **LDC s.192 (proposed use)** for the workshop extension under Class B — confirms PD (~£300-500)
3. **Class B prior notification only** if any new building component — £96
4. **Class R prior approval** if workshop being used for R&D office (if needed) — £536

**Total cost target: under £2,000 vs the £15,090 full planning app the original agent recommended.**

### 5.1 Honest assessment of Irish holding company structures

**Section 110 SPV — NOT SUITABLE.** Built for institutional securitisation/structured finance. A 6.5-acre farm + opticians + MCP business are not "qualifying assets." Skip.

**Irish Private Ltd holding UK subs — only if MEOK is GENUINELY Ireland-managed.** Three reasons:
1. **CFC rules:** UK shareholder ≥25% of Irish co with insufficient Irish substance triggers UK CFC apportionment. Needs real Dublin office, Irish-resident directors making real decisions. €40-80k/yr ongoing minimum
2. **Dividend flows:** UK→Irish dividends 0% WHT; Irish→UK exempt under DTT (if not Irish-resident-controlled) — fine, but doesn't help if CFC applies
3. **Pillar 2 / global minimum tax:** Only bites groups >€750m revenue — non-issue for Templemans

**ICAV / ILP** — institutional collective investment wrappers, inappropriate at this scale. ILP only makes sense >€20m investable wealth + outside LPs.

**Verdict:** No Irish wrapper until MEOK has real Dublin team. Mainstream UK structures cover everything needed.

### 5.3 Critical 2026 facts

- **TRS (Trust Registration Service):** since 2022, almost ALL UK trusts must register within 90 days. Don't try to hide a trust
- **🎯 APR/BPR £2.5m cap from 6 April 2026:** Autumn 2024 Budget headline £1m was REVISED UPWARD to £2.5m (confirmed Dec 2025). 100% relief on first £2.5m of combined APR+BPR; 50% above (effective IHT 20% on excess). Cap is **per person**, transferable to surviving spouse — couple shelters £5m of qualifying assets. Trusts get their OWN £2.5m cap refreshing every 10 years.

### 5.4 Recommended structure for Templeman family

**The cleanest delivery: lifetime Interest-in-Possession (IIP) trust** with:
- **Trustees:** Nick + independent professional trustee (defeats "sham" challenge, protects from care-fee deprivation-of-assets enquiry)
- **Life tenants:** Wendy + Fred (successive — survivor takes whole)
- **Remainder beneficiary:** Nick (+ issue with power to add)
- Parents keep right to live in farmhouse + receive farm income for life
- On second death, trust property is in deceased's estate for IHT → APR/BPR claimed there

**Watch out for:**
- **GROB (Gift with Reservation of Benefit):** if parents continue to live rent-free after gift, asset stays in estate. Legitimate workaround: retain formal life interest in trust itself, OR pay market rent
- **POAT (Pre-Owned Asset Tax)** — interacts with GROB, needs specific advice
- **Deed of variation within 2 years of death** — can retrospectively redirect estate into trust
