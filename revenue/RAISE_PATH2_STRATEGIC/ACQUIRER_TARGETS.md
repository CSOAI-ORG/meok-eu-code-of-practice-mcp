## How to read the ranking

Each target is scored on three axes (H/M/L):
- **Fit** — how directly MEOK's IP plugs a gap they have *publicly committed to filling*.
- **Reachability** — realistic odds a credible warm/cold approach lands a corp-dev or product conversation given MEOK's pre-revenue, sole-founder status.
- **Deal type** — most likely structure: **Asset** (buy the IP/repos/crosswalks), **Acqui-hire** (founder + IP), **License** (tech/data license, founder stays).

The list is ordered by **strategic plausibility for MEOK specifically** — i.e. fit × reachability, weighted toward who has *demonstrated* they buy small AI-governance IP.
