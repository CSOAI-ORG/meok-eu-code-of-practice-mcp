The story

Nick Templeman spent 15 years running Templeman Opticians — a cash-business domiciliary eye care operation across 14 UK local authorities. When AI started touching his patients' lives (falls prediction AI in care homes, AI-assisted diagnostics in NHS), he asked a simple question: where's the signed certificate that says this AI is safe?

The answer, in 2026, was: there isn't one. There are 90-page PDFs nobody reads, Big-4 quotes that take 8 weeks and cost £40-80K, and a regulatory landscape (EU AI Act, DORA, NIS2, UK AI Bill) that's moving faster than the tooling.

So Nick built MEOK. CSOAI Ltd, UK 16939677. 100% Nick. Not VC-backed. Not acquirable. No exit pressure.
