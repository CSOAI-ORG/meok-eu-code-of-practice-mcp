name: business-plan
description: Generate a full business plan for a specific business with market research, competitors, SWOT, financials
---

For the specified business, create a comprehensive business plan covering:

1. **Executive Summary** — What the business does, target market, revenue model
2. **Market Research** — TAM/SAM/SOM, market size, growth rate, key trends
3. **Competitor Analysis** — Top 5-10 competitors, their pricing, features, funding, weaknesses
4. **USP & Positioning** — What makes this business different, positioning statement
5. **SWOT Analysis** — Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats
6. **Financial Projections** — Revenue targets (30/60/90 day), cost structure, break-even
7. **Go-to-Market Strategy** — Distribution channels, content strategy, partnerships, outreach
8. **Agent Topology** — What AI agents should run this business (customer support, content, outreach, monitoring)
9. **Todo List** — Prioritized action items for next 30 days
10. **Key Metrics** — What to track, KPIs, dashboards needed

Use web search for current market data. Save the plan to ~/clawd/business-plans/{business-name}-plan.md

Usage: /business-plan meok-ai OR /business-plan cobolbridge
