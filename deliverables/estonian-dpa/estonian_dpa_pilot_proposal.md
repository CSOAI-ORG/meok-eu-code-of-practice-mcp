## Why Estonia First

Estonia is the ideal first-mover partner for three reasons:

1. **Digital-first governance:** e-Residency, X-Road, and digital government leadership make AKI uniquely positioned to adopt API-first regulatory tools.
2. **Proportional size:** 1.3M population, manageable AI system volume for a pilot, but high visibility as an EU digital leader.
3. **Strategic signal:** Success in Estonia creates a reference for Germany, France, and the European AI Office.

> "Estonia doesn't regulate AI with spreadsheets. Estonia regulates AI with APIs."

---

## The Problem

By August 2, 2026, Estonia must enforce the EU AI Act as a market surveillance authority. This requires:
- Tracking all high-risk AI systems operating in Estonia
- Identifying prohibited practices (social scoring, biometric scraping, emotion recognition)
- Coordinating with 26 other EU Member State authorities
- Issuing warnings, corrective orders, and fines

**Current state:** Most EU DPAs are building this capability from scratch. AKI has the digital infrastructure advantage but still needs the regulatory intelligence layer.

**The gap:** No tool exists that maps AI regulations to Estonia's specific context and provides real-time cross-border coordination.

### What MEOK Provides (At No Charge)

| Component | Description | Timeline |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **Estonia Regulatory Profile** | Complete mapping of Estonian AI obligations (EU AI Act, GDPR, NIS2, e-IDAS) | Week 1 |
| **AI System Registration Portal** | API + basic web UI for operators to register AI systems | Week 2 |
| **Compliance Gap Scanner** | Automated checks for registered systems against Estonian requirements | Week 3 |
| **Cross-Border Advisory** | Automatic flagging when Estonian systems deploy to other EU states | Week 4 |
| **Transparency Register** | Public-facing register of AI systems (white-label, AKI-branded) | Week 6 |
