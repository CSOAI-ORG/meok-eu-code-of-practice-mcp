Council for Safety of AI — the 52-Article Charter for AI Safety.

What CSOAI is

CSOAI is a non-profit (UK Companies House 16939677) that certifies, audits, and rates AI systems for safety. The 52-Article Charter covers:

  • Risk classification (unacceptable / high / limited / minimal)
  • Transparency + watermarking (Article 50 EU AI Act)
  • Bias detection + fairness
  • Audit logging (hash-chained, HMAC-signed)
  • Explainability (Article 13 IFU)
  • Plus 47 more articles...

The MEOK MCP Fleet

CSOAI is operationalized through the MEOK MCP fleet — 340+ Model Context Protocol servers that implement the Charter as composable, MIT-licensed, PyPI-installable code.

Certification

CEASAI (Charter-Enabled AI Safety) certification is available for AI systems that pass a 30-question audit using the fleet. See councilof.ai for the full process.
