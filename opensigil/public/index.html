Not another agent protocol — the layer that proves what they did

MCP, A2A, ACP are the transports — how agents call tools and hand off. OpenSIGIL is the thin signed-audit layer that rides on top of any of them. Whatever your agents use to talk, OpenSIGIL gives you the notarized record of what they decided.

🖋 Signed

Every exchange is HMAC-signed (free) or Ed25519 + RFC-3161 timestamped (paid) and hash-chained — tamper-evident by construction.

👁 Human-readable

gloss() turns any line back into plain English on demand. Dense for machines, legible for humans and auditors.

⚖️ Compliance-ready

A replayable transcript of automated decisions — exactly the evidence EU AI Act Art 12 (logging) + Art 14 (oversight) and ISO 42001 ask for.

🔌 Transport-agnostic

Drop it alongside MCP / A2A / your own stack. One line per decision, one ledger for the fleet.
