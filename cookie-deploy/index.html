Cookie Policy

1. What cookies we use

MEOK AI Labs uses strictly necessary cookies only. No analytics cookies, no advertising cookies, no third-party tracking cookies.

The strictly necessary cookies are: a session cookie (for the sign-in flow), a CSRF token (for form submissions), and a Stripe checkout cookie (only on the Stripe checkout page).

2. What we don't use

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Hotjar, Mixpanel, Segment, or any other third-party analytics. We use self-hosted PostHog (UK region) for product analytics, but it does not use cookies.

3. Local storage

We use browser local storage for: theme preferences, your local AI settings (Maternal Covenant character presets), and the offline verify tool's cache. None of this is sent to a server.

4. Your choices

You can clear all MEOK cookies in your browser settings at any time. The service will continue to work. We respect "Do Not Track" headers.
