FAQ

Is my data private?
Yes. Run fully local with your own model, or use hosted with cross-device memory. "Take it local" gives you a signed export (GDPR Art-20 portability) any time — your characters and memory walk with you.
What does "signed & auditable" actually mean?
Every AI decision writes a receipt to a hash-chained ledger (SIGIL). You can recompute the chain yourself to prove nothing was tampered with. It's in the OS Compliance tab.
How is distress handled?
A care-gate sits on every reply. Signs of distress are always met with real human-support signposting (e.g. Samaritans 116 123 in the UK) — never ignored or amplified.
Can I make my own character?
Yes — the Character Factory lets you design one (archetype, care-style, voice). It persists to your roster across all your devices.
What happens at the free daily limit?
At 50 messages/day you're prompted to upgrade to Pro (£9/mo) for 2,000/day + all characters. Nothing is lost; pick up the next day or upgrade in one tap.
Do you protect against prompt injection?
Yes — a Morris-II-class worm-guard scans agent inputs/outputs and an independent reviewer can reject malicious or self-replicating content before it spreads.
Is there an app?
MEOK ONE is an installable PWA (add to home screen) and a desktop build. A native app-store listing isn't live yet.
