Why the price difference

The price difference is not because MEOK is cheaper-quality. It's because MEOK is differently-architected.

  • Same tooling they use, we built: MEOK built the audit pipeline on its own sovereign infrastructure. Big-4 use junior consultants + their partner's weekly check-in. We use 32 MCP servers + an AI agent.
  • No travel, no slide decks: Big-4 quote 2-3 days per week of consultant time on site + weekly status meetings. MEOK ships the audit + the signed attestation + the public verify URL + the remediation backlog in 4 working days, async.
  • No 90-page PDFs: Big-4's deliverable is a 90-page PDF nobody reads. MEOK's is an Ed25519-signed JSON-LD attestation that survives 7-year retention, verifiable offline with one pip install.
  • Sovereign UK vs EU/US: MEOK stores audit data on UK infrastructure. Big-4 use EU + US data centres, which is increasingly a problem for UK public sector buyers under GOV.UK data-residency rules.
  • Open vs closed: MEOK's 32 MCP servers are MIT licensed. You can fork them, run them yourself, audit the wire format. Big-4's tooling is closed-source.
